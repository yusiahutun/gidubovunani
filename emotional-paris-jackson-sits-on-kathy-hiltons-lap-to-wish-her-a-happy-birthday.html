One might have thought Kathy Hilton had two daughters named Paris.

At her 60th birthday dinner on Wednesday night, Kathy Hilton hugged an emotional Paris who sat on her lap. No, not that Paris.

Paris Jackson was on the verge of tears as she wished Paris Hilton's mom a happy birthday at Mr Chow in Beverly Hills.

The 20-year-old was among the famous faces at the intimate girls-only dinner, along with Kathy's close pal Kris Jenner, half-sister Kyle Richards, and daughters Paris and Nicky.

Each of them delivered speeches praising the popular birthday girl, but it was Michael Jackson's only daughter who appeared most moved by the occasion.

"I love you and I hope you'll always be here because I want to be with you," Paris cooed, as she sat on her knee.

"I love you and you mean so much as a friend," she continued, before diving in for a tight hug, which was reciprocated by a clearly touched Kathy.

Paris Hilton also gushed over her mom, but managed to maintain her composure.

"I love u so much, you're my best friend, you are everything, I wouldn't be the person I am today if it wasn't for you. You've the biggest heart in the world,' she said. "You're so funny, you're so generous, with your time, with your laughs. I literally I don't know what I'd do without you."
