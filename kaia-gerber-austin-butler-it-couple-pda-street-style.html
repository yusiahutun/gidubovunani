Kaia Gerber and Austin Butler Are the It Couple in It Clothes

Kaia Gerber and Austin Butler showed us exactly why they're the It couple of the year during their latest super-candid romantic stroll.

The two were spotted in Los Angeles's Silver Lake this week looking effortlessly cool as ever in outfits made up of all the trendy pieces of the season.

If we were to play a game of fashionable I Spy, we would find some Adidas Samba sneakers, crew socks, a chic trench, a workwear jacket not meant for work, a trucker hat, and oversized sunglasses. And a cute dog! Did we miss anything?

BACKGRID

Butler and Gerber were pictured holding hands as they walked their dog, Milo, in the neighborhood.

The model layered her beige trench over a navy striped sundress and paired it with white-and-navy crew socks and black-and-white Adidas Sambas—an It shoe for an It girl. A minimalist at heart, she accessorized only with her go-to green Celine Ava bag, brown oversized sunglasses, and diamond drop earrings. She left her straight hair down and finished the look with a brownish-nude lip and red manicure.

The Elvis star, meanwhile, opted for his usual Elvis Presley–esque workwear (he often steps out in staples from cool work essentials brand Carhartt) for the outing. His outfit consisted of a teal cropped work jacket (a similar jacket is available at J.Crew) layered over a plain white tee and worn with loose brown trousers and green Adidas Sambas.

Couples who wear Adidas's three stripes together stay together?
