Best florists in London | top florists for luxury blooms

SHOP NOW

Quintessentially British, the iconic, eccentric Moyses Stevens has been in operation since 1876, and now holds a prestigious Royal Warrant – well actually, it has two.

Using only A-grade buds from both Holland and the UK, the brand creates the kind of bouquets that will be remembered long after their life (there's good reason the royals are regulars).

Head to the 'scented' category for something that'll make your space smell as good as it looks, or add on a luxe Trudon candle or bottle of Ruinart champagne to really make someone's day.

Visit Moyses Stevens at:

1 Tenterden St, W1S 1TA

Selfridges, Oxford St, W1A 1AB

188 Pavillion Rd, SW3 2BF
