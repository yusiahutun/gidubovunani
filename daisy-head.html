Daisy Head Bio, Age, Height, Boyfriend, Net Worth, Wiki

Daisy Head is a well-known English actress famously known for her role as Grace in the American Series “Guilt” and as Amy Stevenson in the BBC drama “The Syndicate”. She is currently cast in the role of Genya Safin in the Netflix fantasy drama series “Shadow and Bone” based on the Grisha book trilogy by Leigh Bardugo.

Early Life

Daisy was born on 7th March 1991 in Fulham, London, England. She was born in a famous family as her father Anthony Head is also a well-known actor and musician. Moreover, her grandmother Helen Shingler was also a famous television actress while her uncle Murray Head is a singer and actor. It is not much available about her mother except for her name i.e Sarah Fisher. She grew up in the city of Bath with her older sister Emily Head who is also an actress who is famous for her role in the sitcom “InBetweeners”. The sisters have also worked together in the drama “The Syndicate”.

Daisy has not shared much about her early education but it is known that she attended the co-education boarding school, Kingswood school in Bath, England. She also took training at the Dorothy Coleborn School of dance at the age of 13 to prepare herself to enter the entertainment industry. Belonging to a family of actors and musicians she naturally wanted to become an actress herself and thus pursued the career at a young age. Thus it is believed that she did not pursue college studies in order to pursue acting.

Daisy Head Career

Daisy started her career in 2004 with her debut role in the television series “Feather boy” in the lead role of Kate Barber. The following year, she appeared with her father in the show “Rose and Maloney” in the brief role of Daniella Terry for an episode. In the year 2010, she made her debut in the film industry through the movie “The Last Seven” as Chloe Chambers alongside Danny Dyer. The same year, she also appeared in a CBBC television musical movie “Rules of love” as Daisy in 2010 alongside Jake Roche.

In 2012, she was cast in the recurring role of Sarah in the series “The Proxy”. In 2015, her major breakthrough role came as the lead role of Grace in the murder-drama series “Guilt” produced by Freeform. She series only ran for 10 episodes but was well received and Daisy’s performance was well appreciated. Her career took off when she appeared in the 2016 fantasy romance movie “Fallen” based on a book by the same name as Arriane Alter. The same year she was cast in the underworld movie “Underworld: Blood Wars” in the role of Alexia.

Rise to Fame

In the following years, she appeared in various movies like “Ophelia” 2018, “The ninth” 2019, “Exit eve” 2019, and also appeared in shows like “A Midsummer’s Nightmare” 2017. She appeared in the main roles in the television series “Girlfriends” aired by ITV in 2018 and in the third season of “Harlots” aired on Hulu in 2019 as Kate Bottomley. Recently in 2021, she appeared in the horror film “Wrong Turn” as Edith directed by Mike P Nelson. It was also announced that she was set to appear in the Netflix fantasy series “Shadow and Bone” as Genya Safin. The show is currently airing and is based on the Grisha Trilogy books by Leigh Bardugo.
