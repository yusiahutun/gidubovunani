30 Days of Hymns Perpetual Calendar Laurel Mercantile

  • Made in the U.S.A.
  • 5" x 6.5"
  • Gold spiral bound
  • Free standing easel
  • Heavyweight card stock

30 Days of Hymns is best used as as a desk calendar, kitchen calendar, or reading nook calendar. Filled with rich, uplifting, and encouraging classic hymns, you'll be surprised by a new song each day of the month.

The borders are reminiscent of illuminated manuscripts and draws inspiration from Scandinavian, Russian, and American folk art and stained glass.
