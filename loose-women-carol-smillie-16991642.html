Loose Women REJECTED Carol Smillie from the panel for her tame romantic past

Back in her heyday the queen of TV hoped she could co-present on the daytime ITV chat show – but she never thought she'd be turned down for being happily married.

In a tell-all interview the Scottish lass claimed she didn't make it past auditions because of her boring relationship history.

Carol told to the Express: "It was quite a long time ago, before I left television. I auditioned because I thought I'd have a laugh and that it’d be good to be a part of it.

"I eventually got in to see them and I had this weird interview where the producer said, 'So, tell me about your romantic past', and I said, 'What do you mean?' and he said, 'Well, how many boyfriends have you had?'"

The Wheel of Fortune star continued: "I thought, 'Are you allowed to say that?' It’s ridiculous.

"I told him not many but I said I felt that the current panel, at the time, didn’t have a fair representation of all women."

She added: "I’d been married, at that point, for about, I don’t know, 15 or 20 years, and I said, 'I’m still very happily married and it’s only fair that you have a good mix of women who have had divorces, some who are still married, some who are single, some who are young and some who are old.'

"He was like, 'Hmm… You know they all go on holiday together?' I said, 'Is that a proviso?' and he was like, 'No, but you know.'"

Carol is married to her hubby Knight and they wed back in 1991.

Together the couple have three children and reside in their family home in Glasgow.

Carol concluded: "I didn’t get it, but I thought I don’t really know that I want to be on that."

Loose Women stars are known for spouting their opinions on love, sex and the rest.

Many of them have have messy relationship history including several divorces – 70-year-old Janet Street-Porter has four failed marriages under her belt.

Catch Loose Women weekdays from 12:30pm on ITV.
