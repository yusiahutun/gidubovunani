Who was Jennifer Dulos?

Jennifer was an American woman who went missing in 2019. She was born on 27th September 1968 to Hilliard Farber, a philanthropist, and Gloria Ortenberg, a banker. She had an elder sister, Melissa Irene Farber.

She graduated from Brown University in 1990 before joining New York University Tisch School of the Arts for a master’s degree in writing. Was Jennifer Dulos a blogger? Yes, she wrote for Patch and ran her blog to earn a living as a stay-at-home mom.

Was Jennifer Dulos ever found?

What happened to Jennifer Dulos? She went missing on 24th May 2019. She was last seen around 8:00 am after dropping off her children at the New Canaan Country School. A neighbouring security camera captured her returning home from the drop-off at around 8:05 am.

That same day, she missed two doctor's appointments in New York at 11:00 am and 1:00 pm. The same evening, two of her friends and her nanny reported her missing when they failed to contact her.

The nanny told the detectives that Jennifer had planned to use the Range Rover for the doctor's appointment, not the Chevrolet Suburban, which was missing from the garage.

Detectives conducted a thorough search of the house, where they found blood spatter on the door, floor, one of the garage walls, and the exterior of the Range Rover.

DNA results showed that most blood belonged to Jennifer, apart from blood on the kitchen faucet, which belonged to her husband. In addition to the blood spatter, the detectives found other evidence of Jennifer being a victim of serious assault.

Was Jennifer Dulos' body found?

The mysterious disappearance of Jennifer Dulos prompted the detectives to perform an intensive search as they believed she was a victim of serious assault.

The police searched many properties in and around Farmington without success. On 19th January 2021, the Connecticut State Police Department visited a Mountain Spring Road property owned by Fotis’ real estate company for further search but found nothing.
