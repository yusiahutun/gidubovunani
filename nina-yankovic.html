Who Is Nina Yankovic? Daughter of Musician Weird Al Yankovic

 Posted on February 21, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  858 words  |  Martina Birk

Nina Yankovic is the daughter of Grammy Award-winning artist Weird Al Yankovic and marketing executive Suzanne Yankovic. She is stepping onto a distinctive path, setting herself apart from her famous parents. Known for her dedication to environmental activism, Nina Yankovic is making her own mark in the world, all while being the apple of her father’s eye.

Who is Nina Yankovic

Nina Yankovic was born on February 11, 2003, in the sunny city of Los Angeles, California. She’s currently 20 years old and has embarked on an academic journey that will lead her to a brighter future.

After graduating from high school, Yankovic pursued her education at Williams College, a prestigious liberal arts college nestled in Williamstown, Massachusetts. She is on the path to earning her degree in 2025.

While Nina Yankovic is undoubtedly passionate about environmental activism, her primary focus remains on her studies. Consequently, concrete information regarding her personal net worth is currently unavailable. In contrast, her father, the celebrated Weird Al Yankovic, has a net worth estimated at a remarkable $20 million, reflecting his illustrious career in the entertainment industry.

Weird Al Yankovic doesn’t have any other children, Nina is the only child of her parents. Sometimes, circumstances or personal choices lead celebrity couples to have only one child, and in the case of Nina’s parents, they decided not to expand their family through birth or adoption after welcoming Nina into their lives.

Nina Yankovic’s journey through life began just one day after her parents celebrated their second wedding anniversary. Growing up under their doting care, she inherited her father’s distinctive sense of humor, evident in a playful video he shared to commemorate her 18th birthday in February 2021.

In the video, Nina had a gecko on her face, and with her characteristic humor, she asked if anyone had seen her gecko.. Additionally, her father shared a video in which Nina showcased her musical talents by playing the accordion while wearing whimsical high boots and a baseball cap.


Although her parents had resolved not to have more children, Nina’s family also includes a dog, adding a touch of joy to their lives.

Tragedy struck the Yankovic family when Nina’s paternal grandparents, Nick and Mary Yankovic, were discovered deceased in their Fallbrook, California home in 2004. At that time, Nina was just over a year old. Relatives found them after they had not been seen for an extended period.

Nick was found in a chair in the living room, while Mary was discovered on the bathroom floor. Their deaths were attributed to carbon monoxide poisoning emanating from their fireplace.

Nina Yankovic Parents

Nina Yankovic’s father, Alfred Matthew “Weird Al” Yankovic, is an illustrious figure in the entertainment world. A five-time Grammy Award winner, Weird Al is renowned for creating comedic songs that playfully mock pop culture and often parody specific songs by contemporary musicians. Like his daughter, Weird Al is also the only child of his late parents.

Weird Al’s career has been nothing short of spectacular. He began his musical journey at the tender age of 16 and has since sold over 12 million albums, recorded more than 150 parodies and original songs, and performed in over 1,000 live shows.

His accomplishments include 11 Grammy nominations, four gold records, and six platinum records in the United States. In 2014, his album “Mandatory Fun” debuted as his first number-one album, making him the third artist, after Michael Jackson and Madonna, to have a song in the Top 40 on the Billboard Hot 100 in the same year.

In addition to his music career, Weird Al has ventured into acting, starring in the movie “UHF” (1989) and the television series “The Weird Al Show” (1997). He has also produced satirical films about his own life, namely “The Compleat Al” (1985) and “Weird: The Al Yankovic Story” (2022). Beyond that, the multi-talented artist has made guest appearances and provided voice-acting talents on numerous television shows and web content. He has even authored two children’s books, “When I Grow Up” (2011) and “My New Teacher and Me!” (2013).

Weird Al’s dedication to his daughter was evident when he dedicated the book “When I Grow Up” to Nina. The book achieved notable success, reaching the fourth spot on The New York Times Children’s Picture Books Best Sellers list, solidifying Weird Al as a New York Times best-selling author.

Weird Al is known for his vegan lifestyle, with occasional indulgences in cheese and dairy. He met Suzanne, Nina’s mother, through a mutual friend, and their initial conversations over the phone led to a deep connection. Despite the challenges of a long-distance relationship, they were drawn to each other’s personalities. Eventually, they tied the knot on February 10, 2001, and welcomed Nina into their lives two years later.

Suzanne Yankovic, Nina’s mother, is a marketing executive for 20th Century Fox, a prominent American film production company. Her estimated net worth stands at approximately $1 million.


While Nina Yankovic has chosen to keep her Instagram account private, her father, Weird Al, is quite active on the platform. He frequently shares light-hearted photos and videos, often featuring humorous moments with his beloved daughter.
