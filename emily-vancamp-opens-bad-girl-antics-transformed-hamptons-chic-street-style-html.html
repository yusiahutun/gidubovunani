'I had my moments of rebellion!' Emily VanCamp reveals bad girl antics as she is transformed in Nylon fashion shoot

She plays a good girl with a dark side on her smash hit Revenge and Emily VanCamp insists she too has had her wild days.

But despite being Hollywood's go to actress to play the girl next door, the 27-year-old has revealed as a teen she would go out to nightclubs underage and had her fair share of acting out.

The star has told how there are more layers to her personality as part of a new fashion feature for Nylon Magazine.

Bright babe: Emily VanCamp stars on the September Cover of Nylon Magazine

The former Brothers And Sisters actress opened up as she was transformed from a Hamptons princess to a street fashion maven by the magazine for the September cover.

The 27-year-old revealed she used to sneak out underage to bars when she was living by herself in Canada as a young teen.

Emily who lived in Quebec as a teenaged= ballerina, said she was fully immersed in the artist scene there.

Bad girls do it well: Despite being Hollywood's go to actress to play the girl next door, the 27-year-old has revealed as a teen she would go out to nightclubs underage and had her fair share of acting out

Revenge served hot: The former Brothers And Sisters actress opened up as she was transformed from a Hamptons princess to a street fashion maven by the magazine for the cover

She told the magazine: 'People were very free there. We would go to bars sometimes. I think about how old I was at that time. How on earth were we able to get into these places?'

The Revenge star said while Hollywood may have typecast her as the girl next door, she does not feel like one.

'I wouldn't have labeled myself that way. I just think I had the look to play those characters.

Fashion rebel: While the 27-year-old normally dresses very slick and feminine the shoot gave her a chance to indulge her quirkier style

'I was a good kid, but I definitely had my moments of rebellion.'

The blonde beauty certainly got a chance to rebel in the shoot wearing everything from short shorts with high top kicks to a crazy silk floral baggy jump suit.

Emily - who his currently filming season three of the ABC hit series - said she cannot be more thankful to the people behind Revenge for casting her.

Hard to deal with: As her star is one the rise, Emily is receiving more attention but says she hates being photographed by paparazzi

Previously the Canadian-born actress had played a supporting role in Brothers And Sisters but ABC execs saw her potential.

'They could've hired someone with a bigger name and banked on that. They took a chance on me, and it's brought on a whole new collection of opportunities.'

However, by going from supporting star to the star has come with its costs, namely Emily has found herself the centre of paparazzi attention - especially since she started dating her co-star Josh Bowman.

Sweater weather: In one image the star donned a 3.1 Phillip Lim and a pair of rhinestone cateye sunglasses by Mercura NYC

The 27-year-old is far from happy about that aspect of her new found fame.

'How do you enjoy a moment [with everyone watching]? I see how people spiral out of control.'

Fans of Emily's thriller series will get to see the star back on their screens September 29.

She's back: Emily is currently filming the third series of Revenge where she will reprise her role as Emily Thorne
