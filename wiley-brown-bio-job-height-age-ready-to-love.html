Wiley Brown Job

Based on LinkedIn, Wiley Brown attended Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University. He earned his B.Sc. degree in 2012 in social sciences. He worked as a Special Education Teacher in 2018 in Kingsman Academy Public Charter School. Then, in 2019, he started working as a Physical Education Teacher.

Furthermore, he is also the co-founder of the Chuck Brown Foundation. Wiley and his brother Nekos started it in their father’s memory. The Chuck Brown Foundation works is organized and operated solely for charitable and educational objectives. Purposely, The Chuck Brown Foundation is dedicated to the vision of the Godfather of Go-Go, Chuck Brown, as a way to give back to the struggles he lived through and felt so passionately about. Education, Homelessness, and Re-Entry into Society After Incarceration; operating with integrity for and in the best interest of the participants of the Chuck Brown Foundation Programs, Inc.

The foundation also focuses on furthering education for career, financial, and lifestyle preparation, helping the homeless by offering nutritional food, shelter, and health insurance options, providing job opportunities for the formerly incarcerated to practice and develop skills self-sufficiently, to adapt skills at work, home, and leisure time, in order grow physically, socially, intellectually and emotionally for a successful lifestyle.

Following his father’s footsteps, Wiley also delved into a career in music. He joined the 11-member band that also includes his sister KK.

Before pursuing music, Wiley worked towards his dream to secure a place in NFL. He played as a defensive back at Virginia Tech made it to the NFL draft and was living in Florida training with Chad Johnson and Brandon Marshall.

But in April of that year, Wiley was attacked and seriously injured by a dog. The long recovery and lasting damage spelled the end of his pro chances. Suddenly, he was forced to rethink his life. “For two years, I kind of went through it,” he says. “It was really hard for me to even be around football.”

Wiley Brown was born to his anonymous mother and his father Chuck Brown.

Chuck Brown is a Grammy-nominated aka “The Godfather of Go-Go” who founded the deeply rooted funk and soul sound of Washington D.C. in the early 1970s. He merged with his roots, his love of the blues, jazz, gospel, soul, and African rhythms. With that, Chuck began to develop his distinctive sound.

Chuck’s unique and signature music style won him a place in American musical history. He had a reputation for his legendary live shows, engaging audience participation, and built around “the beat” to create an incomparable continuous party ambiance. Chuck Brown is best known for his hits such as “Bustin Loose”, “Go-Go Swing Medley, “Chuck Baby” and “Family Affair”.

Growing up, Chuck used to perform with his father as a teenager. He occasionally got on stage to rap. After his father died on 16 May 2012, at the age of 75, he continued performing guest-vocal spots with the band just for fun.
