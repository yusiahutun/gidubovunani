Mr Beast reveals sneak peek into $4 million wild video 'bigger than Squid Game'

Mr Beast has taken to his social media to tease his “biggest video ever” as he pushes the boat out once again by including a subscriber from every country on Earth, in his version of The Extreme Olympics.

The YouTube giant is currently the most subscribed individual in the world, and it’s no wonder with his massively entertaining videos with huge production, such as his real-life Squid Game, which reportedly cost $3.5 million to make.

Mr Beast teases ‘biggest video ever’ – Extreme Olympics

Taking to his Twitter, the YouTube giant, teased his “biggest video ever” which is out next Saturday. In the video, the worldwide successful influencer has managed to get a subscriber from every part of the country to compete in the “Extreme Olympics.”

“Next Saturday’s video I had a subscriber from every country on earth compete in my own version of Extreme Olympics… the sets are 2x bigger than our squid game sets and it’s my biggest video ever (here are some random screenshots)” he tweeted.

The pictures included two pictures of the Extreme Olympics set, in one he can be seen wearing a referee shirt, and cap, as the other teases the extravagant set.

The YouTuber had a $4 million budget for the ‘epic’ new video

As the 25-year-old took to the social media site to reveal his new set, fans were in awe as they commented on how excited they were to watch.

Upon seeing and hearing about the video, one fan asked the YouTuber what his budget was for the video, to which he replied: “Over $4,000,000.”

Fans were in shock with the budget for the vid, as one penned: “I can only imagine what you would do with a $100-200M budget.”

“It would be really interesting what he’d come with,” responded another.

One commented: “Mr.Beast is him, the reinvesting he constantly does into his content has reached unmatched levels.”

“I can’t see anyone topping this guy as a YouTuber in our lifetime,” claimed another.
