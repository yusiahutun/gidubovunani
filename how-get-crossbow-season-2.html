How to Get Crossbow Warzone 2 &amp; MW2 Season 2

 Posted on July 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  316 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The crossbow is one of the most iconic weapons in all of history. As such, many games – including FPS titles – include a crossbow in some way or another. Usually, it is a weapon that you can use to make stealth kills from a distance, so it can come in very useful in certain situations. So if you were wondering how to get the crossbow in CoD Warzone 2 and CoD MW2 Season 2, we’re here to tell you everything you need to know about obtaining this weapon.

How to Get Crossbow Warzone 2 & MW2 Season 2

How to Get Crossbow in Season 2 Warzone 2 & MW2

There are two ways to get this weapon. The first method to obtain it is to complete all of the Path of the Ronin challenges. There are seven of these in the new season, with three of them – Integrity (Gi), Honor (Meiyo), and Sincerity (Makoto) already being available. The other four – Compassion (Jin), Courage (Yu), Loyalty (Chu Gi), and Respect (Rei) are going to be unlocked at a later date.

The second method involves purchasing the Tracer Pack: Ballistic Love Bundle. As of right now, this bundle is still not yet available for purchase, but we can expect to be able to buy it very soon. It is going to feature the “Snack” Operator Skin for Reyes, the “Heartbreaker” (an SMG), the “Lovemaker” (a Marksman Rifle), a five-attachment Blueprint for the Crossbow, the “Roses and Riptides” (a Personal Watercraft Vehicle Skin), the “Crush” (Weapon Charm), the “Lovers and Fighters” (Loading Screen), the Cupid’s Upgrade (Sticker), the “Beating Hearts” (Gun Screen), and the “B Mine” (Animated Emblem). As you can see, the entire bundle is Valentine’s Day-themed, and the Crossbow is a great addition here, thanks to the Cupid’s Arrow association most of us have when it comes to this romantic holiday.
