'Good Charlotte' Vocalist Joel Madden Net Worth Details

The rock industry is full of pearls, among which Good Charlotte is an atom. The band was formed by the high school students, especially initiated by Joel Madden and his twin brother, Benji Madden. Currently, it marks twenty-five years of the band and still is giving the mesmerizing hits to this date.

Joel Madden, whose real name is Joel Rueben Combs, is the lead vocalist for the band, while his brother Benji is a lead guitarist and backing vocalist.

Who Is Joel Madden To?

Joel Madden is married to his wife named Nicole Richie, an American Television personality. They exchanged their wedding vows back on 11 December 2010 at Richie's dad Lionel Richie's Beverly Hills estate, and it was a standard in-a-chapel-ceremony. And Lionel Richie surprised her with elephants during the ceremony since Nicole was obsessed with elephants since her childhood.

Madden and Richie said, 'I do' on Lionel Richie's 1986's song entitled 'Ballerina Girl.' Similarly, Madden's father-in-law sang 'You Are' during the wedding reception.

The ceremony was star-studded for sure with around 155 guests, including Gwen Stefani, Gavin Rossdale, Kris Jenner, Khloé Kardashian Odom, Rachel Zoe, Ellen Pompeo, and Pete Wentz. Run-DMC's Rev Run officiated the lavish wedding ceremony while Samantha Ronson served as the bridesmaid.

Joel Madden and Nicole Richie welcomed two children, namely, Harlow Winter Kate (b. 11 January 2008) and Sparrow James Midnight Madden (b. 9 September 2009). The lovebirds have been living a long-term married life of one decade peacefully as of now.

Joel Madden and his ex-girlfriend, Hilary Duff (©: Dailymail)

Before marrying Nicole Richie, Madden dated an American actress, Hilary Duff, who is known for her works on The Lizzie McGuire Movie, A Cinderella Story, and Cheaper by the Dozen. The lovebirds dated for three years from 2003 to 2006.

Joel Madden's Net Worth Details

Joel Madden has a net worth of $25 million, which he derives from his professional music career. Not only music but Madden leads a multi-dimensional career as a record producer, actor, DJ, and UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador.

Joel Madden is widely acknowledged as a Good Charlotte band member, alongside his twin brother named Benji Madden. Similarly, the band features other members, including Paul Thomas, Billy Martin, Dean Butterworth.

Joel Madden with the band member 'Good Charlotte' (©: All Punked Up)

To this date, the musical group has brought seven albums. They released their self-titled debut album back in 2002, and a couple of years later, they followed up with their second album named The Young and the Hopeless. Besides that, they have four other albums, including The Chronicles of Life and Death (2004), Good Morning Revival (2007), Cardiology (2010), Young Authority (2016), and Generation Tx (2018).

Besides that, Joel Madden has appeared in several entertainment projects, including Not Another Teen Movie, and also served as a vocal coach on the Australian reality show, The Voice.
