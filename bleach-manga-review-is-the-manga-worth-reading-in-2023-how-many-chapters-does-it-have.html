Is the Manga Worth Reading in 2023? How Many Chapters Does it Have?

 Posted on April 25, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  552 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

If you’re an otaku or just someone who’s curious about the world of anime and manga, you’ve likely heard of Bleach. Created by the genius mind of Tite Kubo, this groundbreaking series deserves its status as one of the “Big Three,” alongside legends like Naruto and One Piece. Before you dive into its rich universe, there’s a burning question you probably have: How many volumes of Bleach are there? We’ve got you covered.

The Magnitude of Bleach’s Universe

Let’s kick things off with a bang—Bleach is not just another shonen manga. It’s a blend of eye-popping artistry, intricate storytelling, and action sequences that could make even the most seasoned anime aficionado’s jaw drop. It’s the quintessential series that you can’t afford to not have on your shelf.Bleach is a unique story featuring interesting characters, a fantastic plot, and stunning character designs and you can expect nothing but thrills and chills from this masterpiece.

So, How Many Volumes Are We Talking About?

First things first: Bleach is a completed manga series. You won’t have to dread waiting for the next volume to drop. With a total of 686 manga chapters neatly organized into 74 tankōbon volumes, Bleach provides a more than substantial feast for your eyes and soul. The first volume came out on January 5, 2002, and the saga concluded on November 4, 2016. A journey that started over two decades ago can now be experienced in its entirety,so you know upfront how much time and money you’d have to invest in it!

Manga Box Sets: Collect ‘Em All

For the die-hard fans and the collectors, there are three distinct Bleach manga box sets available. The first box set alone comes packed with the initial 21 volumes, a double-sided poster, and a special collector’s booklet. Now that’s what we call a deluxe anime experience!

Your Roadmap Through Bleach

The reading order for Bleach is refreshingly straightforward. Just start with “The Death and The Strawberry,” released on January 5, 2002, and make your way down to the 74th volume, also poetically named “The Death and The Strawberry,” which hit the shelves on November 4, 2016. It’s an incredible journey, taking you from the high school life of Ichigo Kurosaki to epic battles in realms beyond human understanding.

What Makes Bleach So Special?

Let’s not forget what sets this manga apart from the rest. Ichigo isn’t your average teenager—he can see and talk to ghosts. When he meets Rukia Kuchiki, a Soul Reaper, his life takes a turn that nobody could have predicted. The stakes get higher as they both have to fend off Hollows—corrupt spirits devouring human souls—and venture into the Soul Society. It’s not just about the battles; it’s about friendship, courage, and a fight to protect all realms, from the human world to the Soul Society and even Hueco Mundo, the realm of the Hollows.

Final Thoughts

And there you have it—a comprehensive guide to how many volumes of Bleach there are, the box sets you can get your hands on, and a glimpse into the epic story that has captivated millions. So what are you waiting for? Dive in and immerse yourself in the world of Bleach today!
