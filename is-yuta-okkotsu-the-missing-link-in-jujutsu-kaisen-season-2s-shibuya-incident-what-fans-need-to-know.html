A Burning Question in a Searing Arc

Since the arrival of Jujutsu Kaisen Season 2, the air has been thick with questions, whispers, and a whole lot of hype. Unsurprisingly, one of the most asked questions that are burning hotter than a cursed spirit is: “Where is Yuta Okkotsu?” This season packs a punch with the Shibuya Incident arc, a storytelling masterpiece that many say is revolutionizing the anime world. Remember when The Big Three anime were a thing? Well, say hello to The Dark Trio carrying that flag, and within it, the Shibuya Incident arc stands as Jujutsu Kaisen’s pièce de résistance.

“The Shibuya Incident arc is the pinnacle of JJK lore and a rare experience where there’s no telling who is going to come out on the other side.”

High stakes and heart-stopping moments? Check. But even in this crucible, where every character could use a trump card, Yuta Okkotsu remains conspicuously absent.

Yuta Okkotsu: The Sorcerer M.I.A.

Yuta is far from a newbie. His presence loomed large in Jujutsu Kaisen 0, where he was nothing less than the show-stealer, or let’s say, the curse-breaker. This prodigy was Gojo’s secret weapon against Geto back in the day, and fans are dying to know if he’s still the ace up Gojo’s sleeve.

“Everyone wants to know where Yuta Okkotsu is and whether will he be Gojo’s secret ace in the hole against Geto like he was before.”

What’s Up with Yuta Since JJK 0?

JJK didn’t just throw Yuta into the fray; it rolled out the red carpet with a prequel feature film. He captivated fans with his tragic backstory, having initially thought he was cursed by his late friend Rika. Eventually, he ends up training overseas, but not before a monumental face-off against Geto that had fans at the edge of their seats.

“By the end of JJK 0, Yuta Okkutso awakens the true power within him and, shockingly, defeats Geto all by himself.”

Will Yuta Show Up in the Shibuya Incident?

In the current season, Yuta’s been sidelined, still training overseas. The manga suggests he won’t make an appearance in the Shibuya Incident arc.

“As for Yuta, unfortunately, he will be in Africa for most part of JJK’s main story and the Shibuya Incident arc.”

Jujutsu Kaisen has an ensemble of charismatic characters, each with a role to play when their moment under the spotlight comes. The show has smartly evolved from the classic shonen tropes and Yuta’s absence, though palpable, doesn’t weaken the narrative.

Forecast: Yuta’s Return in Future Arcs

Yuta is destined for great things. This Special-Grade sorcerer is not only Gojo’s distant relative but also a descendent of the Big Three Vengeful Spirits. He’s powerful, and there’s no question he will return. When he does, it’s going to be a seismic event in the Jujutsu world.

“It’s evident that Yuta will have a central role to play following the Shibuya Incident arc and will come face-to-face with the formidable King of Curses to have an epic showdown.”

So, even if Yuta Okkotsu’s presence is missing from the Shibuya Incident arc, his absence is a storytelling choice that’s buzzing with potential. Watch out, because when Yuta returns, you can bet it’s going to ripple through the Jujutsu Kaisen universe in a big, big way.
Jujutsu Kaisen Season 2 returned after a short break on 31st August and is currently available to stream on Crunchyroll and Netflix.
