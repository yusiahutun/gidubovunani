Blackpink Jennie's rumored boyfriends list is overflowing with K-pop legends

Blackpink Jennie’s rumored boyfriend list only consists of K-pop legends. From EXO Kai to BTS V, here’s a look at all the K-pop stars who have allegedly dated the Blackpink superstar.

Blackpink’s Jennie Kim is one of the biggest global celebrity superstars of K-pop. The singer has shown her multi-faceted talent not just in music but also in fashion as she has been the global ambassador of luxury brand Chanel since 2017.

Blackpink Jennie’s rumored boyfriends list is overflowing with K-pop heartthrobs

Blackpink Jennie’s boyfriend list includes the biggest heartthrobs of K-pop including EXO’s Kai.

While in recent years, Jennie’s company YG Entertainment has chosen not to comment on the private life of the superstar, fans still got a glimpse of her starry list of alleged beaus, including BTS V.

Jennie and Kai

Jennie and Kai broke the Internet when the EXO superstar’s agency finally confirmed the relationship gushing, “Kai and Jennie have become fond of each other” in January of 2019. The ‘K-pop power couple,’ Jennie and Kai also posted snaps of their Paris on Instagram making fans swoon over their budding love story.

But the dreamy love story of two of the biggest names in K-pop did not last very long. Within a month, Kai’s music label SM Entertainment released another statement revealing, “It is true that Kai and Jennie have recently parted ways.”

Jennie and G-Dragon

Jennie and G-Dragon’s love story made news headlines after an exclusive report from Korean media Dispatch revealed that the K-pop couple have been seeing each other since 2020 and Jennie often visits G-Dragon at home.

While YG Entertainment did not confirm or deny the reports, fans were convinced of their love story from posts G-Dragon’s alluding Instagram posts.

A close source from the YG Entertainment also disclosed: “Jennie’s mother is also aware of her relationship with G-Dragon. She’s thankful that he takes special care of her.”

Jennie and BTS V

After years of shipping Jennie and BTS V together, photos of the two members in Jeju trip surfaced on social media.

While both the labels of BTS and Blackpink chose not to comment on the relationship, fans have found plenty of ‘proof’ of their dating life including the time Jennie and V were spotted together taking a stroll in Paris.
