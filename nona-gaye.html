About

Nona Gaye is a 49 year old American Actress. Born Nona Aisha Gaye on 4th September, 1974 in Washington, District of Columbia, USA, she is famous for being the daughter of the internationally known legendary R&B/soul singer Marvin Gaye and maternal granddaughter of jazz musician Slim Gaillard, in addition to her roles as Khalilah Camacho Ali in the 2001 biographical sports drama film Ali, Zee in the 2003 science fiction action films The Matrix Reloaded and The Matrix Revolutions, Hero Girl (voice) in the 2004 computer-animated Christmas musical fantasy film The Polar Express, Lola Jackson in the 2005 action spy film xXx: State of the Union, and Charlene Frank in the 2005 Christian drama film The Gospel in a career that spans 1992–present. Her zodiac sign is Virgo.

Nona Aisha Gaye (born September 4, 1974) is an American singer, former fashion model, and actress. The daughter of singer Marvin Gaye and granddaughter of jazz musician Slim Gaillard, Gaye began her career as a vocalist in the early 1990s. As an actress, Gaye is best known for her portrayal of Zee in the 2003 science fiction films, The Matrix Reloaded and The Matrix Revolutions.
