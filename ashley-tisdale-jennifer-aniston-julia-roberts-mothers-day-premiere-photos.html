Ashley Tisdale Flashes Her Nipple Pasties at &quot;Mother's Day&quot; Premiere -- Plus, See Jen &amp; Julia's Leg-

Ashley Tisdale made quite an entrance at the "Mother's Day" premiere in Los Angeles on Wednesday night.

The actress opted to go braless for the event, but luckily, she wore pasties to cover up her nipples. She ended up giving photographers a show when she hit the red carpet in a sheer black tank, flared pants and matching leather jacket and a choker.

She didn't seem fazed by the wardrobe malfunction, though -- or just didn't realize it was happening -- check out her fierce expression!

Two other stars to turn heads at the event were Jennifer Aniston and Julia Roberts, who both showed off her killer legs in shorts.

Getty

Aniston went with a black blazer and leg-baring bottoms, which she paired with black pumps, a matching clutch and sleek tresses. Meanwhile, Roberts wowed in white shorts, a coordinated blazer and a black button up underneath.

Do these ladies ever age?!

The former "Friends" star admitted that she was nervous to work with her "Pretty Woman" costar.

When she was asked why she felt intimidated by working with the Oscar winner, Aniston told ET, "Because it's Julia Roberts! Oh my gosh!"

"It's just a different thing when you get to work with them and then you're doing what you both do, [but] together," she added. "I even flubbed in the first take and called her Julia!"

See them in action when "Mother's Day" hits theaters on April 29.
