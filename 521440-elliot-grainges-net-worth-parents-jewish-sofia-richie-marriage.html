Elliot Grainge's biography

Elliot was born on 6 November 1993 in London, United Kingdom. The entrepreneur is 29 years old as of 2023. He holds a British-American nationality and a white racial background.

Elliot Grainge's parents are Sir Lucian Charles Grainge (father) and Samantha Berg (mother). His father is also a household name in the entertainment industry. Charles is the chairman and CEO of Universal Music Group.

Under his leadership, UMG has expanded digitally and internationally through its distribution partnerships with technology companies like Apple, Spotify and Facebook.

His mother worked as a solicitor. Unfortunately, she suffered from a life-threatening complication in 1993 while giving birth to Elliot and slipped into a coma. She never recovered from that condition and eventually died in 2007.

The London native has two half-sisters, Betsy Grainge and Alice, from his father's second marriage to Caroline Grainge. Elliot relocated to the United States in 2009 and later graduated with a bachelor's degree in arts and music from Northeastern University in Boston.

Career

Elliot Grainge began his career while still a student, where he used to manage a bottle service club business for dance and hip-hop venues in Boston. He began his career as a music executive by working at Interscope Records.

He later became the label's vice president. While at Interscope, he collaborated with iconic musicians like Kendrick Lamar, Lady Gaga and Eminem.

In 2016, Elliot founded his own independent record label called 10K Projects. The label is famous for signing renowned music artists like Trippie Redd, Lil Tjay, and Iann Dior. The record company has grown in leap and bound over the years.

In February 2022, the company acquired the music label Homemade Projects. The 10K projects has produced many hit songs, including Mood and Trippie Redd Love Scars.
