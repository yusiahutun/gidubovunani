Jennifer Lawrence Steps Out in London in a Breezy Stone-Gray Suit

Jennifer Lawrence may spend most of her time out of the spotlight these days, but when she emerges, her fashion is a sight to see.

The Hunger Games star stepped out for a day of engagements in London yesterday looking incredible in a breezy, stone-gray take on the modern suit. The stunning wool set by the Row was made up of an asymmetric long top that seemed to be made from a deconstructed suit jacket with an open back, plus matching loose wide-leg trousers.

Bamby Top in Wool

Bamby Top in Wool

She paired the monochrome suit with strappy black leather sandals, a black leather clutch also by the brand, black oval-shaped sunglasses, and a Tiffany & Co. pendant necklace. Her hair was styled in a sleek, straight ’do, and she wore natural makeup and a glossy berry lip to match her raspberry pedicure.

MEGA//Getty Images

MEGA//Getty Images

The actress is in town to promote her upcoming film No Hard Feelings, which will be released on June 23. She attended the premiere for the movie yesterday. To walk the white carpet at Odeon Luxe Leicester Square, she wore an iteration of a glamorous black gown from Dior’s fall/winter 2023 ready-to-wear collection. While the runway version was entirely sheer and featured a midi length, Lawrence wore a maxi version of the dress with black lining under the skirt. She layered it simply over a black bandeau, which was visible underneath the sequin-embellished bodice. She wore the dress with black leather opera gloves, classic black pumps, and an array of silver earring cuffs from Anita Ko and Ana Khouri. Her hair was pulled into a slicked-back high ponytail, and she looked striking in a subtle smoky eye, black cat-eye liner, and a glossy berry lip.
