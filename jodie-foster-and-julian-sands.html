About

British Actor Julian Sands was born Julian Richard Morley Sands on 4th January, 1958 in Otley, West Riding of Yorkshire, England, UK and passed away on 13th Jan 2023 San Gabriel Mountains, Mount Baldy, California, USA aged 65. He is most remembered for his roles as Jon Swain in the 1984 biographical drama film The Killing Fields, George Emerson in the 1985 romance film A Room with a View, Warlock in the 1989 supernatural horror film Warlock, Dr. James Atherton in the 1990 horror comedy film Arachnophobia, Dr. Nick Cavanaugh in the 1993 mystery thriller and body horror film Boxing Helena, Yuri Butso in the 1995 drama film Leaving Las Vegas, Vladimir Bierko in the Fox action drama series 24, Jor-El in the CW serial drama series Smallville, and Yulish Rabitov in the Cinemax action series Banshee. His zodiac sign is Capricorn.

Jodie Foster is a 61 year old American Actress. Born Alicia Christian Foster on 19th November, 1962 in Los Angeles, California, United States, she is famous for Taxi Driver, The Silence Of The Lambs, Sommersville in a career that spans 1965–present and 1965–present. Her zodiac sign is Scorpio.
