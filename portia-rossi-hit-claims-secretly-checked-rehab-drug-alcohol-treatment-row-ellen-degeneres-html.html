Portia de Rossi hit by claims she 'secretly checked into rehab for drug and alcohol treatment after row with Ellen DeGeneres'

Portia de Rossi has been hit by claims she secretly checked into rehab to battle drug and alcohol problems.

In Touch has published a report alleging that the actress entered Passages Malibu in early May after a confrontation with her wife Ellen DeGeneres.

‘They got into an ugly fight,’ a source allegedly told the magazine. ‘Portia hadn’t been happy for a while and was drinking and isolating [herself], and Ellen confronted her.

Scroll down for video

Secret struggle?: In Touch claims Portia de Rossi (right, with Ellen DeGeneres) checked into rehab for drug and alcohol treatment in May

‘Portia lost it and blamed Ellen for driving her to drink because of her controlling ways and said she wasn’t in love with her anymore. Ellen was crushed and screamed at Portia to get out.’

The report claims that the Ally McBeal star checked into a facility a few days later. Representatives for de Rossi and DeGeneres did not wish to comment to MailOnline.

‘Portia entered Passages Malibu rehab facility in early May for a 30-day treatment for drugs and alcohol,’ another source alleged to In Touch. ‘Ellen visited her every weekend to support her while she was there.’

The calm before the storm: Just days before the rehab reports surfaced, the couple were spotted enjoying a romantic vacation in Hvar, Croatia - seen here on Sunday afternoon looking relaxed and happy as they chat with what appears to be a deckhand after hopping off their luxury vessel to enjoy some sightseeing

Just days prior to the rehab reports surfacing, the couple appeared to be making a real go of getting their relationship back on track as they enjoyed a romantic vacation in Europe.

A photo taken by Instagram user sturiddick on Sunday afternoon revealed that the couple are currently vacationing in Croatia.

The image sees the two women looking relaxed and happy as they talk with what appears to be
a deckhand after hopping off their luxury vessel to do some sightseeing on the mainland in Hvar.

Happy couple: Portia is alleged to have sought treatment after a confrontation with Ellen. The pair are pictured on June 19, supposedly after Portia's alleged rehab stint

Indeed, Ellen, 56, and Portia, 41, looked happy when they were spotted out in New York together in mid-June, supposedly after Portia had completed her alleged treatment. Prior to that, the couple were last pictured on May 11.

The insider claimed: ‘They are on their way to repairing their marriage. It won’t be easy. Portia will always be battling these demons. But they love each other.’

According to In Touch, the couple had allegedly been having relationship problems before the reported row and had tried couples therapy.

The calm before the storm?: Prior to being spotted together in mid-June, Ellen and Portia were last seen on May 10

The source claimed: ‘Ellen is domineering and Portia is passive. Ellen always calls the shots, and Portia never complains.

‘She felt like a prisoner in her own home. She went from sipping white wine occasionally to drinking at least a bottle or more a day and topping it off with a pill. It got to the point where she was out of it four nights a week. They’ve had vicious shouting matches — with both of them yelling cruel, soul-crushing put-downs that they both deeply regretted.’

In Touch reports that the source added: ‘Portia often felt like she’d been cut off from her friends because Ellen wanted her all to herself. Portia felt like she let Ellen turn her into the ultimate
Stepford Wife.

Treatment facility: In Touch claims that the Ally McBeal star spent 30 days at Passages Malibu (pictured)

‘Her bottom wasn’t like the dramatic ones you see on a Lifetime movie. It all progressed slowly, and she finally realized she was powerless and not in control anymore.’

Portia has previously opened up about struggling with anorexia as a teenager and young adult, and credits her wife with having saved her.

Ellen and Portia married in August 2008 after almost four years of dating.
