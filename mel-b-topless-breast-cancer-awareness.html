Mel B Goes Topless for Breast Cancer Awareness

Does the "B" in Mel B stand for "Boobs"?

The curvaceous Spice Girl goes topless in the new issue of Cosmopolitan UK, recreating Janet Jackson's famous 1993 Rolling Stone cover -- with Mel's husband Stephen Belafonte providing the singer's hand-bra.

Scary Spice, 37, put her ample chest on display in the mag for the charity CoppaFeel!, a breast cancer awareness organization that aims to promote the early detection of/routine testing for breast cancer.

In the accompanying article, the singer reportedly says "I love boobs. Me and the other Spice Girls are always having a feel of each others' -- Geri came up behind me and grabbed mine the other day!”

Her husband says he gladly volunteers his services for exams as well.

"I'm a boob man so I had absolutely no problem doing this shoot or helping my wife check her breasts."

The issue hits newsstands September 13.
