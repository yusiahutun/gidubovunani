Blake Lively stuns in new Betty Buzz ad but fans notice something else

Blake Lively stunned in an elegant blue suit for a new Betty Buzz ad but many fans have noticed the same thing and it relates to Ryan Reynolds.

Actress and entrepreneur Blake Lively shared a new commercial for her company Betty Buzz, a brand of non-alcoholic drinks, ahead of Amazon Prime Day. Blake sported a gorgeous look in the hilarious ad and many people believe the post includes a subtle nod to her husband and fellow actor Ryan Reynolds.

Blake Lively stuns in new Betty Buzz ad

The Gossip Girl TV actress, 35, sported an elegant blue suit to promote the launch of new Betty Buzz cans ahead of Amazon Prime Day 2023.

The ad features the 35-year-old Hollywood star surrounded by lush greenery and several logs on the ground as she sits on a tree stump with one of her legs over the other.

“I ordered two cans from Amazon,” Blake says in the clip before the cameras show her baffled expression and two toucans next to her. She goes on to drop the F-bomb, looking visibly annoyed, and adds: “Not a f****** toucan from the Amazon.”

Blake’s ad includes a nod to Ryan Reynolds, fans say

While Blake captivated her followers with her elegant look and gorgeous hairstyle, many fans kept repeating the same thing in the Instagram comments. A lot of people claimed that the ad resembles elements from her husband’s commercials for his alcoholic company Aviation Gin.

“This ad has Ryan written all over it,” one person stated, while another chimed in: “You and Ryan are literally becoming the same person.”

However, someone else thinks that Blake is the mastermind behind all of the Hollywood couple’s ads, writing: “I actually think Ryan’s ads are Blake’s ideas.”

“Love how everyone’s crediting Ryan, (love Ryan too) but anyone else realizes Blake is literally just as creatively genius and comedic individually as well?” another fan agreed. “Adore this AD and adore the companies you’ve created.”

Blake’s Betty Buzz company

Blake’s Betty Buzz mixers are available on Amazon for Prime Day, an annual deal event between July 11-12 where customers can get discounts on a range of products.

In another statement, the actress revealed that Betty Buzz has 30 percent off for Amazon Prime customers during the special promotion event.

“Our glass bottles will always be our premium staple, but cans are key for ease, for storage, for parties, you name it,” Blake explained. “And having our cans on Prime is also key for making sure your mom no longer calls you weekly to restock her.”

The celebrity launched the non-alcoholic drink brand in 2021 and the new cans include the flavors Meyer lemon club soda, sparkling grapefruit, and sparkling lemon lime.
