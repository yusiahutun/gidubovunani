Martin Lawrence and fiance Roberta Moradfar appear smitten as they watch the basketball in LA

They've had their fair share of red carpet appearances of late.

But Martin Lawrence, 54, and his fiancée Roberta Moradfar, 38, took the time to relax court side when they sat front row at an NBA game in LA on Sunday. 

The happy couple appeared in good spirits as they laughed and ate their way through the LA Clippers game against Utah Jazz, with the aesthetics nurse practitioner hand feeding her funnyman fiancé. 

Happy couple: Martin Lawrence, 54, and his fiancée Roberta Moradfar, 38, took the time to relax court side when they sat front row at an NBA game in LA on Sunday

The soon-to-be married pair appeared smitten as Martin leaned across to take a bite of some food from Roberta's hand, while his eyes stayed fixed on the court.  

The couple - who got engaged in 2017 - continued to watch on eagerly as the game unfolded, but their smiles soon faded as their team took on a 13 point loss against Utah.
