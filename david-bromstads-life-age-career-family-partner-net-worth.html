Childhood & Education

David Reed Bromstad, known for his work on television, came into the world on August 17, 1973, in Cokato, Minnesota, United States. He was the youngest of Diane Marlys Bromstad (Krueger) and Richard Harold David Bromstad’s four children.

He was raised alongside his three elder siblings: Dean Richard Bromstad, Dynelle Renee Bromstad, and Dyonne Rachael Bromstad. Additionally, his heritage is a blend of Swedish, German, and Norwegian.

After graduating from Wayzata High School, he pursued his passion for arts and design by choosing to become an animator for Disney. He continued his education at Ringling College of Art and Design in Sarasota, Florida, and also started working as an illustrator for Disney.

Career

After beginning his career as an illustrator for Disney, David went on to establish his own business, Bromstad Studio, which specializes in designing imaginative bedrooms for kids. He relocated to Miami Beach with the encouragement of his peers and even appeared on the HGTV show Design Star.

He emerged as the winner of the competition, earning himself a grand prize of a car and the chance to host the HGTV cable network show. In the following season, he made an appearance as a guest judge and offered encouragement to the finalists.

Furthermore, he was the host of three programs on HGTV: HGTV Design Star, HGTV’s Beach Flip, and My Lottery Dream Home. In My Lottery Dream Home, Bromstad assists lottery winners in finding and acquiring their ideal homes. He is also a gifted artist who showcases his paintings in multiple galleries.

Moreover, he has a collection of home décor and accessories sold through Grandin Road and Home Shopping Network (HSN).

Personal Life

Bromstad and former police officer Jeffrey Glasko were in a committed relationship for over a decade. The two began their romantic relationship after meeting at a singles event on Valentine’s Day in 2004.
