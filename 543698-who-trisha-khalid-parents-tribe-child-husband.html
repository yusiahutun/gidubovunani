Who is Trisha Khalid?

Trisha Khalid is an actress and model who has created a profound career in the entertainment industry. She gained fame from her role in the TV series Kovu and now Becky, airing on Citizen TV. On top of this, one of her TikTok skits went viral.

Trisha, whose real name is Fosi Mpole Hamis, was born in Mombasa, Kenya, on December 31, 1994. She is the youngest of six siblings and was raised by a single mother. Not all of Trisha Khalid's parents are alive, as her father passed away while she was young.

The talented actress attended Star of the Sea Primary School and Star of the Sea Secondary School near Mombasa Town, Kisauni Constituency. She also went to college and studied to work as a cabin crew. However, she didn’t practice the profession but went into modelling and acting.
