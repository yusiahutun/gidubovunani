Toff just wore the most ladylike tweed dress and it's surprisingly cheap

The Queen of the Jungle goes for a classic look and we love it

    Georgia Toffolo uploaded a gorgeous selfie on Instagram on Monday evening, and fans went wild for her ladylike tweed dress which was surprisingly more affordable than it looked. The 23-year-old Made in Chelsea star's frock retails at a purse-friendly £39.99 from high street store Zara and is currently available online in all sizes – which is great news for fans of the star's classic and sleek look. The mini dress is made in a pretty bubble gum hue and has two embellished motifs on the front, as well as dainty straps. Boucle is a big texture for spring, so the winner is at the top of her fashion game.

    Toff looked gorgeous in her latest selfie

    She added a coordinating pearl choker by stylish jewellery brand Ela London and wore her trademark blonde hair in a sleek and straight style, with natural makeup and a blush pink lipstick in the up-close shot, which generated lots of love in the comments section. "Wow Toff you are so pretty" one fan wrote, whilst another added "This is the dress I want!"

    Toff's dress is £39.99 from Zara

    The reality star turned TV presenter has quite a varied wardrobe – and flits between both designer and affordable clothes. In February, she wore a heavily-embellished, floor-length grey maxi-dress by Needle & Thread which set her back £950 when she attended the Conservative Party's black and white ball, with 77-year-old Stanley Johnson.

    The shimmering gown was particularly pleasing on the eye as it had an abundance of tiers made from froth tulle and a delicate ribbon waist detail. The dress is actually from the luxury brand’s bridesmaid collection so has a definite feminine stance and ethereal feel to it.
