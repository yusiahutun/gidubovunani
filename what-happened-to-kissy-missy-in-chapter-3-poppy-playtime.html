What Happened to Kissy Missy in Chapter 3 Poppy Playtime

 Posted on April 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  384 words  |  Martina Birk

Kissy Missy, the pink counterpart to Huggy Wuggy, is one of the more popular characters in Poppy Playtime. While she first properly appearead in Chapter 2: Fly in a Web, where she had a minor role, she is heavily involved with the plot of Chapter 3: Deep Sleep. In Ch 3, Kissy Missy helps you on several occassions, and it’s safe to say that you wouldn’t have been able to complete all the various puzzles and obstacles without her help there. Which leaves the ending and her eventual fate even more of a mystery. So what happened to Kissy Missy in Chapter 3 of Poppy Playtime? Well, that’s what we’re here to try and answer. Of course, it should go without saying that from this point on – it’s all spoilers. If you haven’t yet played through Ch 3, you’ve been warned.

What Happened to Kissy Missy in Chapter 3 Poppy Playtime

What Happened to Kissy Missy in Poppy Playtime Chapter 3

At the very end of Poppy Playtime Chapter 3: Deep Sleep, Kissy Missy helps lower you and Poppy Playtime down an elevator. As you and Poppy descend into the darkness, you will hear a blood-curdling scream from a monster. Poppy will cry out for Missy, telling her to hang on and that you are coming to help her. The screen then cuts to black, and the credits roll – signaling the end of Chapter 3.

Does Kissy Missy Die in Chapter 3 or is She Alive for Chapter 4?

If you are wondering does Kissy Missy die in Chapter 3, as of right now – we simply don’t have any definitive answers as to what happened to her. Though, there are several theories that may alude to her eventual fate. It could be that the Prototype got to Kissy Missy and that is what attacked her and that KM is going to be evil in Chapter 4. Another theory is that the antagonist of Chapter 4 is the creature that attacked Kissy Missy and that we are going to see what it is in the upcoming Ch 4. In any case, we are almost certainly going to find out whether or not Kissy Missy is still alive in Chapter 4. Hopefully, we won’t need to wait for it too long.
