Dakota Johnson Nails Lingerie Dressing in a Sheer Bodysuit and Boxy Blazer

Dakota Johnson is officially the latest celebrity to incorporate lingerie dressing into her street style.

The Cha Cha Real Smooth star arrived at Gucci's runway show during Milan Fashion Week today, where she pushed the sartorial boundaries in an outfit that blended the best of NSFW and nine-to-five style.

Johnson wore a black sheer V-cut bodysuit emblazoned with the Italian luxury house's signature Diamante pattern. She paired the statement piece with a black blazer and matching miniskirt, both of which also bore the Gucci logo print. She finished off the luxe look with sheer black tights from Calzedonia, knee-high heeled leather boots in black, and a black iteration of Gucci's Jackie 1961 bag with a green handle and dozens of spiky silver studs.

Alamy

Jacopo Raule//Getty Images

Johnson was last seen carrying the iconic Gucci bag earlier this month, when she stepped out in Los Angeles wearing a denim-on-denim outfit reminiscent of the '70s. At the time, her retro look included a denim chore jacket with a caramel brown collar, flared blue jeans in a lighter wash, a cozy cropped gray turtleneck sweater, and chunky heeled black boots. As for accessories, she opted for a pair of amber-tinted aviator sunglasses.

The 50 Shades of Gray alum previously talked about getting dressed up in a 2021 interview with ELLE UK.

"To go out into the world, do hair and make-up and wear an outfit to look a certain way, it's super jarring," she told the magazine. "So to wear clothes in which I feel like myself is vital. I feel like Ok this is a version of me.
