His dream career came to him when musician Quincy Jones selected him as the first winner of the Sony Innovators Talent Search. After that, he began working with Jones and became known for his 1990 platinum single “The Secret Garden” from Jones’ double-platinum certified album “Back on the Block.” In 1988 he debuted his album ‘In Effect Mode’ which sold over three million copies. For the song, he was nominated for several Grammy Awards and American Music Awards. Additionally, he won an AMA for Best New R&B Artist and Best New Artist from the Soul Train Award.
