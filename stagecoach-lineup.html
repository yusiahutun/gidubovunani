Whos Performing at Stagecoach Festival?

Yeehaw! The Stagecoach lineup is packed with your favorite country artists as well as mainstream artists who are embracing that side. Who’s going to grace the Mane Stage? Here’s what we know.

Stagecoach started in 2007 with headliners George Strait and Kenny Chesney. Since then, the festival has been attended by tens of thousands of fans, with a record 75,000 attendees at the 2018 Stagecoach. Unlike Coachella, which includes genres like rock, pop, indie, hip hop, and electronic dance music, Stagecoach is focused on country music and related genres, like folk, bluegrass, roots rock, and alternative country. The festival is also the highest-grossing country music festival in the world.

This year includes headlining acts from Eric Church, Miranda Lambert and Morgan Wallen, supported by acts like Post Malone, Jelly Roll, Hardy, Willie Nelson & Family, Bailey Zimmerman, Leon Bridges, Diplo, Dwight Yoakam, the Beach Boys, and Wiz Khalifa. Fans can stream the festival using Amazon Prime.

Before Day One of the festival, Post Malone shared a snippet teaser of a potential collaboration with Morgan Wallen. Will they perform the long-awaited song at the festival? We’ll just have to wait and see. “They say teamwork makes the dream work,” Malone wrote in the caption of a TikTok teasing the song.
