Mark Chestnutt Extends Tradition Lives Tour Into 2017

Country fans hoping to catch Mark Chesnutt on the road are in luck, as he is extending his Tradition Lives Tour into 2017. The singer recently released Tradition Lives, his first album of new material in eight years, and is celebrating the album with fans on tour.

“There won’t be any hip-hop, bro-country, dancing around,” he assures in a press release. “It’s going to be fun. A lot of country songs, a lot of shuffle songs and Texas honky-tonk. It could get rowdy, but the music will always be there for you, right in your face.”

Chesnutt's set each night will include his previous chart toppers “A Little Too Late,” "Gonna Get A Life,” “I’ll Think Of Something,” “Goin’ Through The Big D,” “It Sure Is Monday,” “Brother Jukebox” and “I Just Wanted You to Know."
