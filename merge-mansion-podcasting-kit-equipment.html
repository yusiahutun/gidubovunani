Merge Mansion Podcasting Kit &amp; Equipment

 Posted on July 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  342 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Grandma’s Pool Party is the latest seasonal event to grace Merge Mansion. It started on August 18th, 2023, and is going to end on September 3rd, 2023. During that time, you will be able to get many special Grandma’s Pool Party Decorations for the Pool Party area. One of these Merge Mansion items is the Podcasting Kit Level 8, which then allows you to get the Podcast Equipment as well. To help you get the Podcasting Kit & Equipment in Merge Mansion, we are going to explain exactly what you need to do in order to obtain them.

Merge Mansion Podcasting Kit & Equipment

How to Get Podcasting Kit and Equipment in Merge Mansion

Note that you will need to be at least Level 12 to be able to play this event. Next, start playing the event. The first thing that you need to do here is to complete Event Task #1 so that you can get the Fancy Cabinet. Once you have the Fancy Cabinet, it is going to drop Baking Books. These then drop Cake Decorating. By merging them, you get to Cake Tower Lvl 5. This then drops the Birthday Cake. What you need to do next is to feed it the Cake Knife, Bike Helmet, Inflatable Pool Slide, and Elegant Perfume (Lvl 5). Doing this will transform it into the Birthday Party.

The Birthday Party drops Cake Serving, so merge these Cake Slices to get the Podcasting Kit Level 8. And, finally, the Podcasting Kit drops the Podcast Equipment. If you want to keep only the Podcasting Kit, tap it only three times, as it is going to disappear after the fourth tap. You need the Podcasting Kit for these Secondary Tasks – #11 Drinks will be served and #12 And plenty of good food. And the Podcast Equipment is used for ST #13 We’ve got a nice place to dine, #14 The red carpet looks great, #16 We’ll meet to celebrate, #17 And it’ll all be worth it, and #19 Not knowing what’s coming.
