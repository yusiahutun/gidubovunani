At long last, Jordan’s fate is sealed in Soaps.com’s latest Young & Restless spoilers for Monday, May 27, through Friday, May 31. But what will become of Ashley, Jack, Billy, and Michael remains very much up in the air. Read on, and let the latest teasers explain why.

Restless Rant! A dark twist puts Ashley in grave danger! Plus, Victor’s vengeance causes chaos, and much more!

Young & Restless spoilers week of May 27:

Young & Restless spoilers for Monday, May 27:

In Monday’s recap, Jordan learns her fate, and Ashley goes against her alters’ wishes.

When Victor makes a decision about Jordan’s future, you can bet that it won’t be to set her free with a handshake and a genial warning of “Now don’t do that again, missy!”

At her wit’s end and desperate to help son Connor, Chelsea takes matters into her own hands. But what can she do? What?!

Well, we hardly expected them to be cooperative. Ashley’s alters interfere with her treatment.

Young & Restless spoilers for Tuesday, May 28:

In Tuesday’s recap, Adam asks Sally to move in, and Summer hires Claire.

With good reason, Diane worries about Jack’s sobriety. Is he about to relapse and lose his wife in the process?

Sounds like it will be a test of all the progress Chelsea has made in working on herself when she is called upon to make a tough decision.

Though Summer is an Olympic-caliber grudge-holder, she nonetheless gives Claire the benefit of the doubt. #growth

Young & Restless spoilers for Wednesday, May 29:

In Wednesday’s recap, Jill tells Billy she’s ill, Victor builds a wall, and Diane and Jack’s marriage hits the rocks.

The ultimate spin doctor, Victor controls the narrative about Jordan. Which would be the first thing about the madwoman that he’s managed to control, eh?

Speaking of Jordan, already a new mystery is swirling around Claire’s aunt and the one character whose life she hasn’t touched.

It’s damage control time at Chancellor-Winters. Why? Today, Jill shares alarming news with her son, Billy. Find out how <Jill’s bombshell could change everything for Lily/Devon.

Scrubbing in?! Y&R’s ‘new’ doctor isn’t new at all!

Although we were unaware that Sally actually had any power in Genoa City, she nonetheless makes a power move.

Various daytime stars weighed on her dilemma and now Michelle Stafford needs your help: “I can’t decide. I’m leaving it up to you.”

Young & Restless spoilers for Thursday, May 30:

In Thursday’s recap, Diane tells Jacks she’s leaving him, and Tucker has a strange encounter after confronting Audra.

Between her loathing of Jack’s ex-wife and her fears for her husband, it was only a matter of time before Diane gave her better half an ultimatum about Nikki.

Hoping against hope that a trip abroad will shed light on Ashley’s issues, she and Traci arrive in Paris.

Color us intrigued. Tucker has an unexpected encounter. But with whom?

What do we have here? Photos taken on the set hint at a rematch for a beloved supercouple.

Young & Restless spoilers for Friday, May 31:

In Friday’s recap, Summer hires Sally, and Victor fires Michael.
