They're off: Niall Horan had his braces removed on Wednesday, picture here last week just before he visited the dentist

Niall Horan showed fans before and after shots after having braces removed from his teeth and it's clear the One Direction singer is now sporting a smile worthy of Hollywood. 

The 19-year-old singer had his braces put on in December 2011 and ahead of having them removed on Wednesday he tweeted his delight over losing the straightening device.

He posted: 'Morning. ! This is it ! I'm goin to the dentist! I think this is my last hour with braces! Oh how I'm gona miss them ! Naaaaaaat !'

And after having them removed he posted a snap of his old crooked teeth next to his new perfect smile, showing the difference the braces have made.

Niall had clear braces, which are much pricier than the standard metal version most teenagers have.

The singer's teeth aren't only straighter, they are also much whiter than when he found fame and contestants on the X Factor are usually treated to a teeth whitening treatment during the latter stages of the show.

On Tuesday night Miley Cyrus posted a snap of the Irish heartthrob along with American music producer Mike Will and the 20-year-old singer and actress in a recording studio.

Before and after: Niall tweeted this snap of his teeth prior to the braces and now they have come off

Pre-braces: Niall's teeth were uneven and crooked before the orthodontic treatment. Seen here in 2010

When the pics were taken remains a mystery as Niall was performing with his bandmates at the 02 in London on Tuesday night.

The band have several more dates in the capital before moving on to other areas in the UK, as part of their Take Me Home world tour.

On Wednesday Daybreak viewers saw a behind-the-scenes video as One Direction paid a visit to Madam Tussauds to have waxworks of themselves made.

Niall admitted he was was quite surprised when the band were asked to pose and said: 'We were super excited to find out that they were making us and that people felt we were worthy of a figure.'
