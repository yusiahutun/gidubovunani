Calling 911

On Monday morning of 21st January 2013, a man called 911, reporting that he had killed his wife and son. The man, who was not identified until the next day, claimed the following:

I am going to see his house on fire and harm any police officer that comes to the home.

The man was identified as 52-year-old Hans, an off-duty lieutenant from the Las Vegas Metropolitan Police Department in Nevada.

Stand-off with the police officers

When the Boulder City police officer arrived at the crime scene, they saw the lieutenant standing in the doorway of his house. He appeared to be holding a gun in his hands. Unfortunately, Hans was uncooperative with the police and thus went back into the house, threatening to set the house on fire and take his life.
