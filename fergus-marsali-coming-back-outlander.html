Are Fergus, Marsali Coming Back to Outlander? Are Csar Domboy and Lauren Lyle Returning?

 Posted on April 10, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  715 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Outlander still has the Season 7 Part 2 to go, but fans are already looking ahead to the eighth and final season. The farewell season is sure to feature some familiar faces and there’s one couple, in particular, that viewers are hoping to see. Are Fergus and Marsali coming back to Outlander? Keep reading to find out. 

Outlander is Starz’s historical drama based on Diana Gabaldon’s novel series of the same name. The series, which premiered in 2014, follows Claire Randall (Caitriona Balfe), a former Second World War military nurse in Scotland, who finds herself transported back in time to 1743, where she meets Jamie Fraser (Sam Heughan), a handsome Highland warrior. The two eventually get married and start a family together.

Fergus Fraser (played by César Domboy) was first introduced during Season 2. Born Claudel, Fergus is a French immigrant who grew up in a brothel and never knew his biological parents. He was taken in by Jamie and Claire in 1744 and given his new Scottish name. In Season 3, he met and fell in love with Marsali MacKimmie (Lauren Lyle), the eldest daughter of Laoghaire MacKenzie (Nell Hudson). The couple went on to get married, but they moved away and haven’t been seen on the show since Season 6.

The two characters have a large story arc in Book 8 and 9, so fans are hoping that the show catches up with them before it ends. The premiere date for Season 7 Part 2 was pushed back to November 2024, so fans have a ways to go before Season 8, but the wait will be worth it if Fergus and Marsali return. Keep reading to see if the couple will be coming back to Outlander in Season 8.

Are Fergus and Marsali coming back to Outlander? 

Are Fergus and Marsali coming back to Outlander? The answer is yes! On April 1, 2024, the Outlander Instagram account shared a behind-the-scenes video from Season 8, which is currently filming. 

In the video, Caitriona Balfe told the camera: “We finished a week! First week’s done.” Sam Heughan chimed in and said it’s “been fun,” adding that they have a “new cast, new crew, new set.” Balfe then teased, “You know what else we have? We have some old cast.” The video then panned over to César Domboy and Lauren Lyle, who were in costume as Fergus and Marsali.

“I thought we got rid of you,” Heughan joked, to which Domboy responded: “Touched stones and now we’re back,” which is a reference to how Claire time travels. Balfe concluded the video by telling them: “Welcome back!”

Fans flooded the comments section of the post and shared how excited they are to see the couple again. “Yay!! So excited to see Fergus and Marsali back on set!! 🥰🥰,” one fan wrote. Another commented: “Back by popular demand. I love Fergus and Marsali!” The Outlander account also commented on the post and assured fans it was not an April Fool’s Day prank, to which one fan wrote: “Thank god you clarified because my heart couldn’t take it if it wasn’t true lol.”

The last time viewers saw the couple was in the fourth episode of Season 6. They were going through a bit of a hard time as they had just welcomed their son, Henri-Christian, who had been born a dwarf. Kids in Fraser’s Ridge were picking on him so much that the town had become unsafe for them to live in. Additionally, Fergus was struggling to cope with his son’s condition and blamed himself for it; he began to drink heavily and was lacking purpose. Jamie then suggested they move to New Bern and with that, the family relocated and opened up a print shop there.

Fans can’t wait to catch up with this side of the Fraser clan, but unfortunately, it will be quite a while until that happens. the dual Hollywood strikes forced the show to push back the release of Season 7 Part 2 and the remaining episodes will now come out in November 2024. Although an official release date for the final season has not been set, it will likely be in 2025… or perhaps even later.
