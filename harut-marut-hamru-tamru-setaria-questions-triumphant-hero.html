In our Harut and Marut, Hamru and Tamru Genshin Impact Setaria Questions, Like a Triumphant Hero guide, we are going to take you through this part of the quest. Your mission here is to possess different people and use them to trick Setaria. It’s all a part of the Dreams, Emptiness, Deception Archon story quest, so you have to pull it off correctly. That being the case, we’ll show you what the correct answers are for each of Setaria’s questions.

Setaria’s Questions and Answers, Like a Triumphant Hero Genshin Impact

When it comes to Setaria’s questions and answers in the Like a Triumphant Hero quest in Genshin Impact, the first set will be with Nabiya, and the first choice will be between Harut and Marut and Hamru and Tamru. Setaria will ask the names of the two cats chilling nearby. You’ll find the correct choice below.

The next stop is not as much an answer to a questions. It’s more about staying in character, so to speak. So, here’s what you should choose to not arouse any suspicion.

The next person we have to impersonate is Akim. The first step is to tell Setaria what kind of work you’re looking for. If you’ve talked to Akim, you’ll know what the correct answer is. If not, well, here you go.

  • Uh, gardening work.
  • Uh, masonry work.

The second step of Setaria’s questions portion of Genshin Impact Like a Triumphant Hero involves you picking the option which confirms the story of the stall vendor. Otherwise, Setaria will get suspicious. The vendor said that Akim’s dad hurt his legs, so it’s obvious what you need to choose.

  • He’s feeling a lot better, and he’s walking more now.
  • He’s feeling a lot better, he can lift stuff now.

The last person Setaria will “interrogate” is Qishan. Fortunately, you only have one choice to make here. Qishan studies in the basement, not the attic. So, that’s what you should select.

  • No, I’ve been spending all my time studying in the basement.
  • No, I’ve been spending all my time studying in the attic.

So, that completes the Setaria Questions Part of the Like a Triumphant Hero quest in Genshin Impact. From here, you’re on your own. Things are gonna get plenty interesting, but that’s up to you to discover.
