The MTV VMAs Return To New York For 2024

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) — MTV announced on Wednesday that the MTV Video Music Awards will return to New York for their 2024 edition.

The awards gala, which celebrates music with performances, awards, and tributes, will take place on Sept. 10th at UBS Arena in Belmont.

The show will be broadcast across MTV’s suite of content channels, including its linear television network and its digital platforms in more than 150 countries and territories.

“It’s an honor to host MTV and the VMAs at UBS Arena,” said Mark Shulman, Senior Vice President of Programming, UBS Arena. “This is the culmination to bring a world class event to a venue that offers state of the art capabilities and the best in fan amenities. We look forward to welcoming this year’s top artists, fans, and viewers worldwide to experience our arena and campus at Belmont Park.”
