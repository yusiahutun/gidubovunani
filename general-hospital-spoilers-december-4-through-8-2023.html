General Hospital Spoilers December 4, 2023 Through December 8, 2023

 Posted on August 12, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  701 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Soaps.com has the latest, new General Hospital spoilers from Monday, December 4, to Friday, December 8. Laura probably won’t score any points with Nikolas when she once again seems empathetic to Esme’s situation. Meanwhile, Cody lends a hand to two ladies in need, Cyrus crosses paths with Ava, and it looks as if Brook Lynn and Chase have some news they want to share…

General Hospital spoilers week of December 4:

General Hospital spoilers for Monday, December 4:

In today’s General Hospital recap, Brook Lynn accepts Chace’s proposal, Blaze and Kristina share a kiss, and Molly and TJ make a decision.

Brook Lynn once again proves herself to be our kinda gal when she can’t help but look at a situation through skeptical eyes. Meanwhile, Ned turns to Lois — not Olivia — when he needs to confide in someone!

How will Carly and Joss feel upon learning what Drew has planned?

Who’ll bend to the other when Sonny and Nina wind up disagreeing?

Molly hopes that TJ will listen to her idea with an open mind.

Jon Lindstrom expresses ‘so grateful’ for ‘the wonderful journey’ that’s given him 30 years in Port Charles.

General Hospital spoilers for Tuesday December 5:

In today’s General Hospital recap, Brennan is revealed to be the new WSB director, and Blaze tells Kristina about her fears.

Cyrus might want to brace himself, because he’s about to deal with Hurricane Ava!

Laura once again seems to be Esme’s biggest supporter, offering Ace’s mother reassurances when needed.

Curtis goes to Sonny with a few pointed questions.

Blaze opens up to Kristina.

Dante tells Sam what he’s discovered.

His life in pictures: As Maurice Benard’s son turns 19, look back at his evolution from cute kid to rising star.

General Hospital spoilers for Wednesday, December 6:

In today’s General Hospital recap, Laura threatens to send Cyrus back to jail, Esme and Ace move out, and Sasha and Cody get big news.

Will the mother/son bond grow closer or be stretched to the breaking point when Elizabeth and Jake open up to one another?

Alexis may not yet have her license degree back, but she’s there to offer advice when Finn and Martin need it.

Josslyn wants to know more about Adam’s family.

Things get tense between Spencer and Esme. Or should we say “tenser”?

Dante and Jordan have a bit of information to share with Laura in her mayoral capacity.

Inga Cadranel (ex-Harmony) shares a devastating loss: “The world has lost a light.”

General Hospital spoilers for Thursday, December 7:

In today’s General Hospital recap, Anna and Dante make headway in her arson case, Brennan asks Carly out to dinner, and Portia tells Curtis about a new experimental treatment that could help him walk again.

Dante presents Anna with what he believes to be pretty definitive proof.

Why does Carly find herself so intrigued by someone who wanders into Kelly’s? Maybe it’s because it’s this handsome devil.

Scott and Lucy get caught up in memories of the past as they reminisce about what was.

Scarlett Fernandez (Charlotte) undergoes surgery to remove tumor.

Portia is extremely excited about the possibilities offered by an experimental procedure… but will Curtis have the same level of enthusiasm?

Oh dear. Gregory, Alexis and young Violet are about to share a moment that can only be described as “awkward!”

Alum Briana Lane (ex-Brook Lynn) pays a heartfelt tribute to her late husband on their wedding anniversary.

General Hospital spoilers for Friday, December 8:

In today’s General Hospital recap, Tracy returned to town, Chase and Brook Lynn shared their news, and Molly asked Kristina to be her surrogate.

Will the update Anna provides Valentin help get their derailed romance back on track?

Molly and T decide to take things to the next level by making someone an offer.

What’s the big announcement which Brook Lynn and Chase are ready to make?

Lois and Olivia may not see eye to eye on everything, but they’ll probably find themselves grateful when Cody is on hand to help.

Tracy finds herself reflecting on the past.
