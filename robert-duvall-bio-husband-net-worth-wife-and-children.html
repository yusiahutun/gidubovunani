Hollywood has deployed many distinguished performers and actresses of high repute who have left indelible marks in the world of entertainment. Robert Duvall is a seasoned actor and director. Robert has proven himself in transitioning from supporting roles to leading roles and has managed to gain millions of fans worldwide.

The Hollywood legend has appeared in several classic movies, including MASH, To Kill a Mockingbird and The Godfather I & II. Robert’s unique ability to fully immerse himself in the roles assigned to him has earned him prestigious awards and accolades in the entertainment industry, including a National Medal of Arts presented to him by President George W. the biography

Robert Selden Duvall was born in San Diego California on the 5th of January 1931. His father, William Howard Duvall, had an established career in the military and retired at the rank of Admiral. Her mother, Mildred Virginia, made her living working as an amateur actress. An interesting fact to note is that Robert’s mother is a direct descendant of Robert E. Lee, a character Robert portrayed in Gods and Generals in 2003. The veteran actor was raised alongside two brothers – John Duvall and William Duvall.

He attended Principia College, Illinois for his tertiary education. At Principia College, he majored in liberal arts. After graduating in 1953, Robert Duvall joined the army for a few years before enrolling at the New York Neighborhood Playhouse, where he studied for two years under the tutelage of Sanford Meisner. During his years at Neighborhood Playhouse, he met Dustin Hoffman and Gene Hackman.

wife, wife and children

The Hollywood superstar has recorded four marriages and three divorces. His first attempt at marriage was to Barbara Benjamin whom he met on the set of the film Kill A Mocker. The duo tied the knot on 31 st December 1964 and remained together for eleven years but sadly the marriage ended in 1975.

Robert was single for almost seven years after his first marriage ended, but in 1982 he married his second wife, Gail Youngs. After their marriage, their marriage lasted four years before falling apart in 1986, leading to Robert’s second divorce. The iconic actor’s three nuptials were to his longtime girlfriend, Sharon Brophy, a former dancer he dated from 1989 to 1991. Their relationship, which culminated in a 1991 wedding, continued until in 1996 before ending in divorce.

Robert Duvall’s current wife is Luciana Pedraza who he dated seven years before their marriage on October 6th , 2004. The couple started living together in 1997 before getting married in 2004. A remarkable aspect of this union is that Luciana is 41 years younger than Robert, but they seem to be happy together despite their age difference. They regularly appeared together in public and embarked on several charity projects as a couple.

Although Robert Duvall was married for four years, the veteran thespian did not father his own biological child, but he does have two stepdaughters, Nancy Horn and Suzan Benjamin, from his past marriages.
