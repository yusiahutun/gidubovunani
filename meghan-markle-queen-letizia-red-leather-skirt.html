Meghan Markle and Queen Letizia Wear the Same Red Leather Skirt

Duchess Meghan and Queen Letizia are officially style twins.

Meghan, Duchess of Sussex, was photographed in a red lambskin pencil skirt on Friday as she attended an engagement at Windsor Castle. Meghan hosted a roundtable discussion for the One Young World Summit, accompanied by husband Prince Harry.

The popular item also caught the attention of Spain's Queen Letizia, who wore the skirt during her trip to South Korea with King Felipe VI this week. Understandably, both royals looked impossibly chic in the BOSS by Hugo Boss leather skirt, which is still available to shop, and comes in a number of different colors.

The Duchess of Sussex completed her outfit with a V-neck sweater by Joseph, Sarah Flint's Perfect Pumps in cabernet suede, and a pair of Emily Mortimer's Hera Diamond and Gold Studs, which feature ethically sourced diamonds.

This isn't the first time that Meghan has worn this BOSS skirt design, either. The duchess wore a dark green version of the lambskin piece back in October 2018 during a visit to Brighton with Prince Harry. Clearly, it's becoming somewhat of a royal favorite, and it's easy to see why.
