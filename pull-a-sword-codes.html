How to Redeem Pull a Sword Codes

If you want to redeem the Winter Update January 2024 codes in Pull a Sword, the system will vary slightly depending on whether you’re trying to redeem a Twitter code or a YouTube code. Either way, the first step is to open Roblox and launch the game. Once you’re in, take a look at the right side of the screen. Above the “Claim Gift” button, you’ll see four icons. The two on the right both say “Codes” but one has a YouTube icon and the other the blue check mark from Twitter (I still refuse to call it X). So, depending on which code you’re trying to redeem, click or tap the appropriate button. Then simply enter one of the codes into the text box and hit the “Verify” button. Easy as pie.
