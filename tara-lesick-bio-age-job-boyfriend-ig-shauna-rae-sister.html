Meet Tara Lesick, Shauna Rae’s Sister

Tara Lesick is a biological sister of Shauna Rae from TLC’s reality show “I Am Shauna Rae” in, translation they share the same parents. Tara and Shauna were born to their father Scott Lesick and mother Patricia “Patty” Lesick. However, their parents got divorced and are now happily married in different relationships.

Tara’s biological father Scott is a graduate of Deer Lakes High School. He studied at ATC (AIR TRAFFIC CONTROL) in U.S. Navy and graduated in 1988. His about section on Facebook reads, “just a guy who likes to spend time with family and friends, and fun in life, U.S.Navy 1983-1988, Air Traffic Controller U.S.S. The Coral Sea”.

Scott is currently married to Kimberlee Swidrosky-Lesick who is a brilliant singer.

As for her mother, Patty married their step-father Mark Schrankel and is celebrating their 19th wedding anniversary in 2022. Mark is a brilliant father and he is loving and kind to all four of his kids. Mark congratulated Tara for graduating college in 2018.

But every once in a while, he also loves to share some questions about Tara on his Facebook. Tara Lesick Job

Tara Lesick started her career as an Academic Tutor at William Peace University in January 2016. She served at Red Lobster in 2016. While there, Tara also trained and coached employees by having them follow and watch my techniques and answer their questions. After working for one and a half years, she joined the Autism Society of North Carolina as an intern in December 2017.

Five months later, Tara completed her internship at the Autism Society. In July 2017, she had started working as a server in Nordstorm.

Then, in August 2018, Tara joined Urban Ministry Center as an intern. After four months in December 2018, she quit her job at William Peace University after working as a tutor for three years. During that time, she taught over 50 students in Pre-Law and Psychology by expanding classroom knowledge based on the individual student’s needs.

In March 2019, she completed her internship at Urban Ministry Center.

Currently, Tara has been working for Care.com as Childcare Provider. She started working in childcare in January 2014. She is also working as a research assistant and teacher assistant at the University of North Carolina at Greensboro.

Moving to her education, Tara graduated high school from North Hill High School. While in high school, she was on the school’s softball team. She also has a max prep profile.

Tara earned her BA degree in Psychology and Pre-law in 2018. She earned her Master’s Degree in Experimental Psychology from the University of North Carolina at Greensboro in 2021.
