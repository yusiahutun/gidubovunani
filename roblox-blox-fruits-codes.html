For those who have a fondness for colorful pirates who eat magical fruit to gain unique powers, you’ll likely have heard of Roblox Blox Fruits. It’s a twist on the One Piece manga and animation show where players can eat devil fruit to gain new abilities. You can choose to take on these abilities or become a master swordsman where you take on diverse enemies and engage in boss battles, choosing to fight as a pirate or team up with the government as a marine.

To help you along your journey, we have prepared lists of Roblox Blox Fruits codes, both active and expired. This way, you can get an edge over the opposition, level up faster, earn rewards, and more. Do check back to see when we’ve added more new codes for Blox Fruits.

How to redeem codes in Roblox Blox Fruits

Redeeming your Blox Fruits codes is similar to other Roblox titles. Follow these simple steps, and you’ll be ready in no time:

  • Log into the Blox Fruits game.
  • On the left side of your screen, you will find a Twitter icon. Click on it.
  • Copy and paste or type in the codes.
  • Click on the ‘Redeem’ button and any active codes will be automatically added to your account.
