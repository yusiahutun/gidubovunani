Stray Kids fans 'can't breathe' as Felix strips off shirt and reveals back tattoo

Stray Kids star Felix is here to make fans gasp for air as he strips his shirt off for his solo stage exposing his tattoo!

Dubbed as K-pop’s fourth-generation trendsetters, Stray Kids are having yet another legendary year as they tour through the world gifting fans one after another epic and unforgettable stages. On August 16, the South Korean boyband performed at Fukuoka as part of their 5-STAR Dome Tour, and Stays (as Stray Kids fans are called) met with many surprises!

Stray Kids fans ‘can’t breathe’ as Felix strips off shirt and reveals back tattoo

Stray Kids star Felix knows how to turn heads on every possible occasion. Arguably one of the most charismatic performers of K-pop, the singer has a lot more to offer than just his deep voice and his recent solo stage is proving exactly the same.

Felix took the stage on August 16 at Stray Kids’ concert for his solo performance and surprised fans with a bare back exposing his tattoo! The reactions were priceless as Stays all over the world took to social media swooning over the iconic moment.

Stays say this Felix will go down on history

Stray Kids fans are not over the Felix moment even hours after the concert ended as the star trended on Twitter with thousands of Stays speaking of his impact.

One excited fan posted on Twitter: “Felix rips his shirt” becoming true, I can’t breathe!”

“This performance is insane, the dance, the lyrics, the stage presence… Felix is really born to be on stage. I’m so proud of him,” says another fan.

Only within a few years of debut, Stray Kids have shown immense progress and the concert stages are becoming milestones of their incredible transformation. Speaking of the growth, a third fan gushed: “this dome concert feels very like a making a point that they’ve grown up, does anyone get what I mean? Like with the solo stages very personal to each member’s own style, Felix going shirtless, swearing at the top of their lungs, you get me?”

Another fan spoke everyone’s mind tweeting:

Felix makes his debut on Billboard Japan Hot 100 Lyricists

Stray Kids members are not just singers and dancers but also gifted songwriters. After 3RACHA (Bang Chan, Changbin, HAN), Felix becomes the fourth member to debut at Billboard Japan Hot 100 Lyricists and fans think, this is only the beginning!
