Meet Brittany Mattessich Parents: Anthony And Anna Mattessich!

Get to know Anthony and Anna Mattessich, the parents of Brittany Mattessich. Are they still married? What do they do for a living? How old are they? Do they have any other children?

Scroll down and learn more about Brittany’s folks.

Who Are Brittany Mattessich Parents?

Brittany Mattessich came from a family of four who are based in Paramus, New Jersey. Her parents are called Anthony Mattessich and Anna Mattessich (nee Busanic).

Anthony and Anna had Brittany in September 1989 as the youngest of two children. Brittany is a social media influencer and reality star who recently got engaged to her fiance Frank Catania Sr.

Brittany shared, “We’re Engaged!!!! 💎🤍 I’m so excited to start this next chapter with you along with all the other good things we have going on. Thank you for being you & making me the happiest girl in the world. I wouldn’t want to do this life with anyone else other than @frank.catania.sr 🤍🤍 love you so much my fiancé 🤍🤍🤍.”

In the photos, Brittany flashed the massive diamond ring she received from Frank while they kissed. The Bravo celebrity proposed in front of a floral wall filled with red flowers and a banner that asked, “Will you marry me?”

Frank and his now-fiancée’s relationship had been on and off for years, but they eventually made things official in May 2022 before he got down on one knee. Before their friendship turned passionate last year, she and Frank were friends for over ten years. He trained her for her fitness competitions, which is how they originally got together.

Her fiance Frank is known for marrying Dolores Catania, the star of RHONY. They were married from 1994 to 1998. The exes shared two children Gabby, aged 28, and Frank Jr., aged 25.

“Congrats on the engagement! @missbrittyann. It’s so nice to officially welcome you to the family 🥰,” Dolores excitedly said about the engagement. “Wishing u a lifetime of love, health and happiness @frank.catania.sr. Happy wife happy life.”

Meet Anthony Mattessich, Brittany Mattessich Father

The late Anthony Mattessich is the father of Brittany Mattessich. Before his retirement, he was employed at H and M International in S. Kearney, as a Teamster in Local 560.

On Father’s Day 2018, Brittany wished her father, “Happy Father’s Day to this gem 👼🏼 this special angel of mine ❤️💭💭… there are not enough words to describe just how important my father was to me 💔❣️… Thank you for always guiding me and watching over me !! I love you so much and miss you sooooo much ❤️❤️ love your little princess… Happy Father’s Day to all the daddy’s out there 💋.”

Later, in September 2018, Brittany wished her dad his heavenly birthday, as he wrote, “Happy Birthday to my sweet angel 👼🏼🙏🏻❤️… there’s not a day that goes by that I don’t think of you ..(I miss your hugs and kisses and all the caring things you did for me ) hope heaven is throwing a big party for you because no one deserves it more 😘 🎉🎁🎈… love you more than you will ever know ❤️ #missyou #daddyslittleprincess.”

When Anthony passed away on 12 November 2014, he was 55 years old.

Right after Anthony passed away, Brittany shared the heartbreaking news on her IG. She wrote, “We little knew that day, God was going to call your name. In life we loved you dearly, In death, we do the same. It broke our hearts to lose you. You did not go alone. For part of us went with you, the day God called you home. You left us beautiful memories, Your love is still our guide. And although we cannot see you, You are always at our side. Our family chain is broken, And nothing seems the same, But as God calls us one by one, the chain will link again. 💕👼 I will forever be your little princess 💕🙏 R.I.P. Daddy.”

Meet Anna Mattessich, Brittany Mattessich Mother

Brittany Mattessich’s mom is named Anna Mattessich. Anna’s maiden name is Anna Busanic. Anna turned 62 in March 2023. It is not clear what she did for a living.

Brittany has featured her mom on her socials a few times. On Mother’s Day 2018, Brittany took to IG and wished her mom, “Happy Mother’s Day to this beautiful gem of mine 👸🏼 my #queen Anna🍌 ❤️ my bestest friend & my world 🌎… sometimes I wonder how you put up with me then I remember I put up with you, so we even 🤗🤪 love you to the 🌙 &🔙.”
