Robert Alai Pleads with Well Wishers to Help Bright Needy Boy Join Form 1: &quot;Every Little Bit Counts&quot;

  • Kileleshwa MCA Robert Alai urged compassionate Kenyans to support Meshack Wandera in his pleas to join high school
  • Alai disclosed that Wandera scored 370 marks in KCPE and earned admission at Sigalame High School in the recent placement
  • The MCA stated that the family was not in a position to help Wandera further his studies, despite his impressive results

Nancy Odindo, a journalist at TUKO.co.ke, brings more than three years of experience covering politics, news, and feature stories across digital and print media in Kenya.

Kileleshwa member of the county assembly, Robert Alai, is rallying netizens to unite and offer support to a former Kenya Certificate of Primary Education (KCPE) student facing a dim future because of financial difficulties.

Meshack Wandera, a talented and promising student, achieved an outstanding score of 370 marks in his KCPE at New Hope Maranatha Educational Centre and has been granted admission to Sigalame High School.

He was among the students who surpassed the average mark of 250.

"We are reaching out to all compassionate hearts in our community and beyond to join our initiative to send Meshack Wandera to school. The Wandera family is facing financial hardship and is unable to cover the costs of Meshack's education at Sigalame High School. Despite their best efforts, they find themselves in a difficult situation that threatens to deny Meshack the opportunity to continue his studies," he said.

He also said that there would be proper records of all monetary donations and that he would regularly update everyone on the progress of the initiative.

Why should Wandera be supported?

The MCA highlighted Wandera's demonstrated capability to excel and pave the way for a promising future.

Despite his academic prowess, the family's financial challenges and the weight of school fees might hinder his enrollment in the prestigious school.

Alai further emphasised that education serves as the gateway to a brighter future and asserted that no student should be deprived of opportunities due to financial constraints.

"We believe that education is the key to a better future, and no deserving student should be denied this opportunity because of financial constraints. Education is a powerful tool that can transform lives, and by supporting Meshack's education, you are not only investing in his future but also the betterment of our community," he added.

How to channel contributions

Alai requested compassionate individuals to direct their monetary and in-kind donations to his office via an M-Pesa number, acknowledging and valuing their efforts.

"Your generous donations, whether monetary or in-kind, will go a long way in ensuring that Meshack can continue his education and work towards his dreams. To contribute monetarily, please use the M-Pesa number: 0708677607.In-kind donations can be channelled to the MCA's office through the same M-Pesa number: 0708677607. Every little bit counts and brings us one step closer to helping Meshack realise his educational aspirations," he advised.
