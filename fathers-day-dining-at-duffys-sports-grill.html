Fathers Day Dining at Duffys Sports Grill

Treat Dad to a Father’s Day feast at Duffy’s Sports Grill all weekend-long, featuring a selection of mouthwatering barbecue entrees.

The weekend’s specials include a half or full rack of barbecue ribs starting at $17.49 and $26.99, respectively; a half rack of ribs and wings starting at $21.99; and a half rack of ribs and shrimp starting at $22.99. To wash it all down, Dad’s first pint of Miller Lite, Blue Moon, or Yuengling is on the house.

Guests can take advantage of Duffy’s Father’s Day food specials by dining in, taking out, or ordering delivery. Stop by the Stuart locations (6431 SE Federal Hwy. and 1 SW Osceola St.) to indulge.
