Loeffler Randall Bridal Shoes Collection

Media Platforms Design Team

Loeffler Randall has the "something new" angle covered for brides-to-be. Since she began the line in 2005, designer Jessie Randall explains, "countless friends and editors have asked me to help them with their wedding shoes because they could not find the styling they were looking for." After outfitting three different friends for their nuptials this past season, Randall decided to add five wedding-appropriate styles. The shoes will be available in February and range in price from $295 to $625.

Véronique Hyland is ELLE’s Fashion Features Director and the author of the book Dress Code, which was selected as one of The New Yorker's Best Books of the Year. Her writing has previously appeared in The New York Times Magazine, The New Yorker, W, New York magazine, Harper’s Bazaar, and Condé Nast Traveler.
