Celebrity Family Feud: Jenna Dewan hilariously fails TWICE while competing with cast of The Rookie

Jenna Dewan hilariously failed while competing with the cast of The Rookie on Sunday's episode of Celebrity Family Feud on ABC.

The 42-year-old actress got nervous after host Steve Harvey, 66, asked her to 'name an athlete who has so many trophies, he'd make a good trophy husband.'

'You got this. You got this,' said her co-star Mekia Cox, 41.

'I don't know! I don't know!,' Jenna said in high-pitched panic. 'Um, Kareem Abdul-Jabbar.'

Jenna burst into laughter and turned away as Steve made a funny face over her answer.
