Ellia English (born March 26, 1960) is an American singer, dancer, stage and film actress. She is best known for her role as Aunt Helen King on the comedy sitcom The Jamie Foxx Show which originally ran from 1996 to 2001 and as Mary Lou Wentz, the mother of Teddy's best friend Ivy on Good Luck Charlie from 2010 to 2014. English was featured in a central role on the former, and was paired onscreen with Garrett Morris, who plays her husband Junior King.
