Mercy Masika on How Walalo 'Neighbours' Cooking, Perfume Affected Her Pregnancy Journey; &quot;I'd Puke&quot;

  • Renowned gospel singer Mercy Masika revealed her challenging experiences during her first pregnancy
  • The mother of two narrated how she had to sleep on the floor of her sitting room due to a weird smell in her bedroom
  • The singer explained how the smell of her cupboard, onions, and blanket triggered immediate nausea

Gospel singer Mercy Masika has shared the difficulties she encountered during her first pregnancy.

Weird smell in bedroom

In an undated video captured from a past YouTube interview, the musician revealed she had a rough time due to an aversion to certain smells, which made her unable to use her bedroom.

"When I smelt my cupboard I would throw up immediately, so I couldn't go to my bedroom because my bedroom had a weird smell. It was my cupboard and my blanket. They smelled in a particular way as soon as I entered my bedroom I would throw up," she said.

Masika described how the smell of her cupboard and blanket would trigger immediate nausea, forcing her to sleep on the floor of her sitting room.

Additionally, the presence of strong smells from the neighboring Walalos (Somalis) cooking garlic and using unfamiliar perfumes added to her discomfort.

"We were living upstairs and there were Walalos (Somalis) who were living there and they would do garlic and they had some perfumes that were not very familiar. The smell was very intense. If I entered home, I had a problem. If I knew about masks I would have worn them. First trimester, I was sleeping in the sitting room on the floor because I couldn't be near the window. I had to sleep on a mattress with a particular bed sheet," she said.

Masika asks married couples to avoid temptation

Previously, Masika advised married people to be strong and courageous and not be easily carried by people's looks.

The Shule Yako hitmaker said there will always be people who look better and cuter and it's okay to appreciate beauty but that should not be the reason one steps out of their marriage.

"You will always find attractive people wherever you go. Whether you are married or you are single, attractive people are always going to be there. Being strong and courageous is saying, well you are cute or handsome but I am strong and courageous in the Lord, to be strong in my marriage or singlehood" she said.
