“I get a lot of comments saying my kids are going to get bullied when they are older,” she exclusively told Daily Star.

“I find it vile they wish a child to get bullied. My kids won’t get bullied though as it’s 2023 and you can’t get away with bullying people.

Morgan’s favourite content to create is X-rated videos and photos in public places, such as the gym.

“He can’t exactly be proud or not when he’s been dead for nine years.

“Nobody has ever said anything to me in person, which just shows what society has become where people think they can say what they want without consequences.

“I’ve got numerous people in trouble with their work because I send the screenshots of abuse to their employer.”
