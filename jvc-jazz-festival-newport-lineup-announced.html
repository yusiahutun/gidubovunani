JVC Jazz Festival-Newport Lineup Announced

Diana Krall, Ray Charles and Natalie Cole will headline the 2001 JVC Jazz Festival Newport. Krall will headline at the opening night gala on Friday evening, Aug. 12 and perform again at Fort Adams State Park on Saturday, Aug. 11 with other performers to be announced. Cole and Charles will appear at Fort Adams on Sunday, Aug. 12.

Early ticket sales will take place on the Internet and at the Festival office only at a date to be announced shortly.
