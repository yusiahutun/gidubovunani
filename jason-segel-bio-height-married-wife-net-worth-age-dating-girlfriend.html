Jason Segel Bio (Age)

Jason Segel is from California and was born on 18. January 1980. He is one of Alvin and Jillian Segel’s three children. Jason and his siblings had a very comfortable upbringing near Malibu, California, as their father was a lawyer and their mother was a homemaker.

Jason attended Harvard-Westlake School. Despite being quite tall and athletic in his youth, Segel had other interests. His inclination towards the performing arts was evident as a teenager when he started making his own films.

It was during this time that his talents caught the attention of a Hollywood executive, and Jason made a life-changing decision to drop out of college to pursue an acting career.

career

Jason Segel debuted on the acting scene as an 18-year-old after winning a role on Freaks and Geeks . Choosing to work both behind and in front of the camera, Segel wrote and starred in the 2008 rom-com Forget Sarah Marshall . The actor has since established himself as a comics performer and accumulated other screen credits. His other notable credits include Knocked Up (2007), the 2009 film I Love You Man , two comedies in 2010 – Take Him to the Greeks and Gulliver’s Travels, Bad Teacher (2011), The Five Year Engagement (2012) as well 2015 The End of the Tour .

Segel’s many endeavors in the entertainment industry have been recognized both locally and internationally. Notably, he received the 2011 Critics Choice Award for Best Song for The Muppets’ “Life’s a Happy Song.” Other awards include the 2011 Chicago Film Critics Association Lifetime Achievement Award and the 2014 Scott Brown Memorial Award.
