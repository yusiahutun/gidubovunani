Why Hong Kongs Jeffrey Ngai looks better than his AI counterpart: inside the actor, singer and lu

Notably, actress Fish Liew commented, “You look even better in real life,” echoing the sentiment of many.Beyond his modelling career, Ngai’s fashion sense, poise and ability to connect with audiences have made him a highly sought-after brand ambassador for luxury labels. This was evident when he caused a stir on social media after attending the Louis Vuitton Men’s spring/summer 2024 fashion show in Paris, mingling with stars like JJ Lin, Song Joong-ki, Got7’s Jackson Wang and Bam Bam.

Ngai’s talents extend into acting as well. He is a regular on ViuTV’s Be on Game, showcasing his versatility and natural on-screen charisma. He’s often compared to actor Edison Chen.

His appeal lies not just in his good looks, but also his myriad of styles. Let’s delve into his dynamic catalogue of looks, from nautical and colourful ensembles to leather and denim.

Aye aye, captain!

If you’re ever on a turbulent voyage, simply think about Ngai’s sailor and captain looks to calm down. He effortlessly plays with brightly hued fabrics, adding depth and complexity to his appearance and proving that style and functionality can go hand in hand.

Suited up

Ngai strikes an artful balance between classic and contemporary styles with his fashion versatility. He added a modern edge to his loose-fitting blue suit from Louis Vuitton with a patterned shirt, while his preppy style from Dior exudes a clean vibe with a grey suit jacket and jumper together with earth tones.

Flower boy

For an evening party, he channels his edgy side with a sleek Dior outfit featuring an embroidered cardigan with a plunging neckline and statement accessories.

Offering a breath of fresh air with bold hues and whimsical motifs, Ngai plays the role of a flower boy with confidence and flair. He pulls off a summery vibe that is perfect for airport fashion – a look that Mirror’s Anson Lo knows a thing or two about, too – wearing an aqua-toned LV x YK monogram faces shirt over white, as he prepares to attend Paris Fashion Week for menswear in June.

Bold in leather

Wearing leather from head-to-toe, Ngai dons a blue Prada double-breasted leather trench coat with combat boots and a leather belt bag, masterfully pulling off the daring combination of ruggedness and sophistication.

He also adds his own twist to collegiate fashion by leathering up with a Miu Miu brown jacket over an argyle knit cardigan and shorts.

Denim days

When it comes to the timeless wardrobe staple denim, Ngai’s take showcases his fashion-forward sensibility. He pairs vintage-inspired Calvin Klein dungarees with a sharp red top and Balenciaga letter necklace, creating a bold and unexpected yet tasteful combination.

In another snap, he exudes approachable, casual cool in Gucci’s monogrammed greyish-black print denim jacket and jeans.

Ngai’s knitwear looks are a testament to his sweet and innocent aura. He was seen sporting a cosy style in the MV of his most recent solo, which he produced as a special gift to himself on his 25th birthday to encourage his fans to stay true to themselves and embrace their unique personalities, he said on his Instagram.

In January, he attended the reopening of Chloe’s store at Pacific Place and wore a similarly cosy top. His Chloe zipped wool and cashmere-blend cardigan is a delightful mix of stitches and colour-blocking that results in a striking graphic aesthetic.
