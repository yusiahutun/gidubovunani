Who Are Late Chef Howie Kleinberg Family Members?

Howie Kleinberg who was the star of “Top Chef” passed away over the weekend. She told Miami Herald, “I am just finding out how many lives he touched,” she said of her son, who owned and ran Bulldog Barbecue in North Miami for 10 years.”.

His funeral was held on Tuesday morning surrounded by his family and friends. Along with his immediate family, his Top Chef family was also saddened to learn about his untimely passing. Howie appeared on the show in season 3 after  Terry Zarikian, culinary director of the South Beach food festival, recommended him for the Bravo cooking competition series. He finished the show in seventh before getting eliminated.

“The Top Chef family is saddened to learn about the unexpected passing of Howard Kleinberg from season three,” Bravo said in a statement. “Howie’s passion for cooking filled so many and our deepest sympathy goes out to his family and friends”.

Four months before the unprecedented event, Howie had started working at the Coconut Grove, Florida, restaurant the Peacock Garden Resto Bar + Grill as an executive chef. “We at Peacock Garden are saddened by the passing of chef Howie Kleinberg,” the restaurant said in a statement. “Howie was a valued member of our team and we extend our deepest sympathies to his family”.

Howie worked to promote charitable organizations near and dear to his heart during his illustrious career as a chef, which started in the kitchen of the Hotel Intercontinental in Miami. He contributed to “Taste of the Nation,” which raises money to fight childhood hunger. In addition to owning a rescue dog named Skye at the time of his passing, he also campaigned to raise money for animal shelters.
