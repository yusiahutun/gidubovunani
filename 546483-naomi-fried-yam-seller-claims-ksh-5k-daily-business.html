Naomi: Fried Yam Seller Claims She Makes KSh 5k Daily From Her Business

  • Naomi, a self-driven food vendor from Akyem Oda in Ghana's Eastern Region, has candidly opened up about her business
  • In a heartfelt interview, she revealed earning an impressive GH₵500 (about KSh 5,000) daily from selling fried yam despite the challenges she faces
  • Naomi's ambitious plans for her savings include a five-year target to invest her hard-earned money into opening a saloon

Naomi, a Ghanaian fried yam vendor who relocated from Akyem Oda in Ghana's Eastern Region to Accra for greener pastures, has proudly disclosed that she makes GH¢500 (about KSh 5,000) daily.

During a candid interview on Ghanaian Grind on 3Xtra, the determined businesswoman opened up about her life and food venture.

Why Naomi moved to Accra for a better life

Naomi disclosed that she moved to Accra due to joblessness in her native town, where life was difficult due to financial reasons.
