Travis Kelce knows 'exactly' how he got Taylor Swift to fall in love with him

Travis Kelce is once again gushing about his relationship with Taylor Swift, and he’s throwing it back to where it all began – and how he got her to fall in love with the star.

The celebrity couple first went public in September, after Taylor Swift was spotted cheering Travis Kelce and the Kansas City Chiefs on at Arrowhead Stadium. However, the NFL star has revealed the music star wasn’t into sports at all before she met him. But he does know exactly how he got her to fall in love with him, despite having no interest in his profession…

Travis Kelce ‘knows exactly how’ he wooed Taylor Swift – it’s all love

We can always count on Travis and Jason Kelce‘s New Heights Podcast to give us all the tea on our favorite relationship. After all, it was the first place we found out about Kelce’s ‘friendship bracelet’ antics, in an attempt to get to know the singer.

This week, the brothers have rapper Lil Dicky on the sofa, and it seems he’s just as big a fan of the relationship as we are.

When asked about the fans and the relationship, Kelce replied, “I’m having a blast in life baby. Just flying high, enjoying it all, bringing new lives to the football world.”

“I don’t know how I did it, because she wasn’t into sport,” the 34-year-old said. “So I don’t know how the f*** I did it.”

However, Swiftie Lil Dicky knew exactly how, as he began to recite the whole story of Trav ‘calling her out’ on his pod.

“Yeah, I know exactly how I did it,” the tight-end cheekily said while looking into the camera, before laughing and shyly looking away.

Taylor’s thoughts on sports have ‘drastically changed’ since then

It looks like the tables have definitely turned, as Tay seems to be the Chiefs’ number one fan, as last season we saw her cheering and gulping beer from the stands.

From wearing Travis Kelce merch to flying all the way from Tokyo to Vegas to make it in time for the Super Bowl, there’s no going back for the Bad Blood singer now.

However, the support isn’t one-sided, as the NFL star has traveled across the globe to see his girlfriend on tour, including Sydney and Singapore, where they were seen on a date at the mall.

But we know he’s been a Swiftie at heart all along!
