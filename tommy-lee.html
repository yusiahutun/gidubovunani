Professional Life and Career

Initially, Tommy debuted with the band “Suite 19” in the 1970s. In the year 1980, he together with members,  Nikki Sixx, Mick Mars, and Vince Neil formed the band,  ‘Mötley Crüe’.

The albums of the band are  ‘Too Fast for Love’ in 1981, ‘Shout at the Devil,’ ‘Theatre of Pain,’ ‘Girls, Girls, Girls,’ and ‘Dr. Feelgood’.

In the year 1999, he left the band and created another band, ‘Methods of Mayhem’. His debut solo album was ‘Never a Dull Moment’ (2002). In 2004, he reunited with his former band.

In the same year, he published his autobiography, ‘Tommyland’. Also, he gave his appearance in a television show, ‘Tommy Lee Goes to College’ in 2005.
