The Rookie: Who is Nathan Fillion dating? Get the details

 Posted on August 15, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  465 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Nathan Fillion has had quite the television career. After making a name for himself on hit dramas such as Desperate Housewives, Firefly and Castle, he is now known for his role in the ABC drama The Rookie.

But while we all know a lot about his character John Nolan, what is there to know about Nathan himself when the cameras are off? Find out more about Nathan Fillion's dating history and current love life below...

Who is Nathan Fillion dating?

The actor, who has now starred in the ABC drama for over five years, is currently believed to be single. The Canadian-American actor has never been married, but he has been linked to a number of co-stars and other actors over the years and has even been engaged three times.

For now however, Nathan appears content with the company of his close friends as he continues to go from success to success within the acting industry.

Who has Nathan Fillion dated in the past?

The 52-year-old actor has been linked to several women, and as previously mentioned, has had three fiancée's over the years.

Krista Allen

Nathan was most recently linked to George Clooney’s ex, Krista Allen, who he is thought to have dated in 2015. According to reports, Nathan and the former Anger Management actress hit it off on the set of the ABC series Castle when she made a guest appearance.

Vanessa Marcil

Nathan popped the question for the first time to his General Hospitals co-star Vanessa Marcil back in 1995 after eight months of dating. However, the couple were together for just over half a year before they called it quits and went their separate ways.

Perrey Reeves

The actor then dated Entourage actress Perrey Reeves for four years between 2005 and 2009 before falling for Mikaela Hoover, who he proposed to just months into their relationship. The pair began dating in April 2013 and were engaged before the year was out. Sadly, it wasn’t meant to be and the two split not long after.

Mikaela Hoover

Nathan got engaged for a second time after dating Guardians of the Galaxy star Mikaela for several months. The pair met and got engaged in 2013, but split before getting married in 2014.
