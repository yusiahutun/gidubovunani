Aug 5, 2024 Aldo Pusey

Goombay Bash

Andrew Dady, Megan Looby, Alex Romlegh and Rebecca Van Dyke

The highly anticipated summer event took place in the Fairwind Air Charter airplane hangar at Witham Field. Hundreds of guests filled the dance floor and enjoyed specialty drinks and gourmet treats from the area’s best restaurants as acrobats hung from the rafters, performing silk routines. Funds raised were used to help in purchasing vital equipment necessary for the treatment of children and infants and support the Mother Baby Home Care program.
