New Films Coming to Hallmark Channel

 Posted on June 24, 2024  |  12 minutes  |  2551 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

  • Hallmark’s 2024 Movie Schedule

    2024 is bringing a slate of new movies to the Hallmark Channel, including several fun romances that fans are sure to love and new releases for Hallmark Movies & Mysteries. 

    The year kicked off with four new romances and a new mystery titled Family Ties, the latter of which Nikki DeLoach wrote and starred in. The network then honored Jane Austen with a month of original movies inspired by the iconic writer. The programming event, titled Loveuary with Jane Austen, began on Feb. 1 and featured original romances like Paging Mr. Darcy. 

    Throughout the month of June, Hallmark will air its Passport to Love movie event, which boasts four new premieres starring Andrew Walker, Bethany Joy Lenz, Hunter King and more. Each film transports viewers to a dreamy European location and features the perfect summer romance. 

    Hallmark will then kick off its 12th annual Christmas in July celebration. The lineup includes two new premieres, Falling Like Snowflakes and A Very Vermont Christmas, as well as the television premieres of Rescuing Christmas, An Ice Palace Romance and Three Wise Men and a Baby: Extended Cut, which were previously only available to stream on Hallmark Movies Now. Viewers can also vote on which past holiday movies they want to air in the Hallmark Movie Checklist App.

    Later in the year, longtime collaborators Lacey Chabert and Brennan Elliott will reunite for a new movie set to premiere during the network’s annual Fall into Love event. “We are so excited to reunite Hallmark’s perennial fan-favorite Lacey Chabert and Brennan Elliott in this all-new movie,” Ayse Francis, Hallmark’s Director of Programming, said of the Crossword Mysteries co-stars. “Three years having passed since they’ve appeared together on screen, this witty and playful romcom will showcase their undeniable chemistry – a treat for our Hallmark viewers.”

    When Calls the Heart‘s Erin Krakow and Daniel Lissing will also be reuniting for the first time since Lissing left the hit show in 2018. The pair will co-star in the Christmas movie Santa Tell Me as part of Hallmark’s Countdown to Christmas event. 

    Below, find the full list of Hallmark’s new movies for 2024.

  • Love on the Right Course

    Premiere Date: Saturday, January 6, at 8 p.m. EST.

    Cast: Ashley Newbrough, Marcus Rosner, Brittany Bristow, Steve Byers

    In Love on the Right Course, Ashley Newbrough stars as a professional golfer who returns home to Budapest to reevaluate her career after a series of setbacks. Once there, she meets pro golfer Daniel Balázs (Marcus Rosner), who helps her find herself again. 

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • A Scottish Love Scheme

    Premiere Date: Saturday, January 13, at 8 p.m. EST.

    Cast: Erica Durance, Jordan Young

    Erica Durance stars as Lily in A Scottish Love Scheme, a woman who travels to Scotland with her mother and reconnects with a childhood family friend, Logan (Jordan Young). Unbeknownst to Lily and Logan, their mothers have been meddling with their love lives and developing an elaborate plan to set them up.

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • True Justice: Family Ties

    Premiere Date: Friday, January 12, at 8 p.m. EST.

    Cast: Katherine McNamara, Markian Tarasiuk, Nikki DeLoach, Benjamin Ayres

    The first Hallmark Movies & Mysteries film of the year stars Katherine McNamara, a precocious law student named Casey, who sets out to prove that her brother was wrongfully convicted. Co-writer Nikki DeLoach stars as Professor Ambrose, Casey’s law professor. 

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Movies & Mysteries

  • Betty’s Bad Luck in Love

    Premiere Date: Saturday, January 20, at 8 p.m. EST.

    Cast: Laci J Mailey, Marco Grazzini

    Betty’s Bad Luck in Love follows a woman who has been cursed since she was a child to never find love. When Betty (played by Laci J Mailey) meets Alex (Marco Grazzini), however, she is tempted to give love another shot.

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • Romance With a Twist

    Premiere Date: Saturday, January 27, at 8 p.m. EST.

    Cast: Jocelyn Hudon, Oliver Renaud

    Romance With a Twist takes a classic love story to new heights. It stars Jocelyn Hudon as Luna, a former dancer who pairs up with world-class aerialist Bennett (Oliver Renaud) after he finds himself without a partner. As the two prepare to showcase their skills at a local arts festival, a romance begins to brew.

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • Paging Mr. Darcy

    Premiere Date: Saturday, February 3, at 8 p.m. EST.

    Cast: Will Kemp, Mallory Jansen

    Paging Mr. Darcy will mark the first movie of Hallmark’s Jane Austen month. It stars Mallory Jansen as an overly serious scholar who attends a Jane Austen annual conference and strikes a deal with the man playing Mr. Darcy (Will Kemp). The pair’s love of Jane Austen books allows them to overcome their pride and admit to their belief in love.

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • Love & Jane

    Premiere Date: Saturday, February 10, at 8 p.m. EST.

    Cast: Alison Sweeney, Benjamin Ayres

    Love & Jane centers on hopeless romantic and literary enthusiast Lilly (Sweeny), who gets the surprise of a lifetime when Jane Austen appears in her life. 

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • An American in Austen

    Premiere Date: Saturday, February 17, at 8 p.m. EST.

    Cast: Eliza Bennett, Nicholas Bishop

    In An American in Austen, Harriet (Bennett) is transported into Pride & Prejudice and gets to find out if a real man can compare to Mr. Darcy himself. 

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • Sense and Sensibility

    Premiere Date: Saturday, February 24, at 8 p.m. EST.

    Cast: Deborah Ayorinde, Bethany Antonia, Dan Jeannotte

    In this adaption of Sense and Sensibility, Marianne is torn between two men, while Elinor longs for a man beyond reach. 

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • Shifting Gears

    Premiere Date: Saturday, March 23, at 8 p.m. EST

    Cast: Tyler Hynes, Katherine Barrell

    In Shifting Gears, Katherine Barrell stars as Jess, a female mechanic who reluctantly agrees to participate in a car restoration show. She is shocked to learn that her ex-boyfriend, Luke (Tyler Hynes), is her main competitor. Will sparks reignite?

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • An Easter Bloom

    Premiere Date: Saturday, March 23, at 8 p.m. EST

    Cast: Aimeé Teegarden and Ben Hollingsworth

    Virgin River star Ben Hollingsworth appears alongside Aimeé Teegarden in An Easter Bloom, which follows a young gardener as she sets out to save her family farm by entering a floral competition for Easter. She meets a local pastor along the way who helps her restore the hope she lost.

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • One Bad Apple: A Hannah Swensen Mystery

    Premiere Date: Friday, April 5 9/8c

    Cast: Alison Sweeney, Victor Webster 

    The ninth addition of the Hannah Swensen Mysteries franchise is here, and it will reunite The Wedding Veil and Days of our Lives co-stars Alison Sweeney and Victor Webser. In One Bad Apple, baker-turned-sleuth Hannah Swensen (Sweeney) meets a new side of law & order as she investigates a murder and crosses paths with by-the-books prosecuting attorney Chad Norton. 

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Movies & Mysteries

  • Blind Date Book Club

    Premiere Date: Saturday, April 6, at 8 p.m. EST

    Cast: Erin Krakow, Robert Buckley 

    Sparks fly with When Calls the Heart star Erin Krakok and Robert Buckley in Blind Date Book Club. A bookstore owner finds love and direction in life after agreeing to review a famous author’s new novel in her blind-date-with-a-book club. 

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • Legend of the Lost Locket

    Premiere Date: Saturday, April 13, at 8 p.m. EST

    Cast: Natasha Burnett, Viv Leacock

    When an antiques expert takes on a quest to find a long-lost locket that promises to bring the wearer true love, she finds herself in conflict with, but also attracted to, the town sheriff.

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • Falling in Love in Niagara

    Premiere Date: Saturday, April 20, ay 8 p.m. EST

    Cast: Jocelyn Hudon, Dan Jeannotte.

    After her fiancé leaves her before their wedding, Madeline goes to Niagara Falls to honeymoon without him. There, she reconnects with her adventurous side, learns to let go, and finds new love.

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • Curious Caterer: Foiled Plans

    Premiere Date: Friday, April 26 9/8c

    Cast: Andrew Walker, Nikki DeLoach

    The fourt installment of the Curious Caterer franchise will see caterer Goldy Berry (Nikki DeLoach) team up with Detective Tom Schultz (Andrew Walker) once again to solve a new food-related crime. When Goldy agreed to cater Tom’s ex Jessamyn’s elaborate wedding, she didn’t expect a dead groom and missing bridezilla would bring her together with Tom again, to catch a killer and find Jess. 

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Movies & Mysteries

  • Branching Out

    Premiere Date: Saturday, April 27 8/7c

    Cast: Sarah Drew, Juan Pablo Di Pace

    To help build a family tree, single mom Amelia (Sarah Drew) tracks down her daughter’s biological father (Juan Pablo Di Pace). It becomes a journey of trust, love, and discovering the meaning of family.

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • A Lifelong Love

    Premiere Date: Saturday, May 4 8/7c

    Cast: Andrea Brooks, Patch May

    While trying to reunite her grandfather with his lost love, Annika (Andrea Brooks) teams up with college sweetheart Ryan (Patch May) to create a book about the experience, documenting different love stories along the way.

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • A Whitewater Romance

    Premiere Date: Saturday, May 11 8/7c

    Cast: Cindy Busby, Ben Hollingsworth

    Maya (Cindy Busby) attends an exclusive business retreat in the Rocky Mountains where she meets Matt (Virgin River‘s Ben Hollingsworth) who, initially, is her competitor. As they’re forced to pair up, an unlikely connection begins to evolve.

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • Family Practice Mysteries: Coming Home

    Premiere Date: Friday, May 17 9/8c

    Cast: Amanda Schull, Brendan Penny

    A former Army surgeon moves back to her hometown and joins a practice of family doctors, but when she’s drawn into solving the mysterious death of a patient, her quiet life becomes upended.

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Movies & Mysteries

  • Everything Puppies

    Premiere Date: Saturday, May 18 8/7c

    Cast: Pascal Lamothe-Kipnes, Stephen Huszar, Kathryn Davis

    A dedicated entrepreneur and inventor looking to make it big creating innovative dog toys and treats finds success with the support of a handsome client.

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • Big Sky River: The Bridal Path

    Premiere Date: Saturday, May 25 8/7c

    Cast: Emmanuelle Vaugier, Kavan Smith

    The sequel to 2022’s Big Sky River, Big Sky River: The Bridal Path catches up with Tara, who is now settled in Montana and dating Cowboy Boone. She works to bring their family lives together ahead of their wedding but tensions arise, as blending their families will be more of a challenge than anticipated.

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • For Love & Honey

    Premiere Date: Wednesday, June 5 9/8c

    Cast: Andrew Walker, Margaret Clunie

    Beekeeper Eva uncovers an ancient fresco while rescuing a hive. Austen, a visiting archeologist, thinks it is key to his research, so he persuades Eva to help him on his quest across Malta. 

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • Savoring Paris

    Premiere Date: Saturday, June 8 8/7c

    Cast: Bethany Joy Lenz, Stanley Weber

    Disillusioned with her life, Ella embarks on a soul-searching journey to Paris where she navigates love, self-discovery and cheese amidst the enchanting backdrop of The City of Love. 

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • A Recipe for Greek Romance

    Premiere Date: Saturday, June 15 8/7c

    Cast: Danielle C. Ryan, Rafael Kariotakis

    After a recent setback, Abby heads to Greece to visit her mom and decide what’s next. While there, she meets Theo and they team up to open a restaurant. But will Abby be able to stay?

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • Two Scoops of Italy

    Premiere Date: Saturday, June 22 8/7c

    Cast: Hunter King, Michele Rosiello

    When an American chef travels to a quant village in Italy for inspiration, she falls in love with the flavors, culture, gelato, and the Italian gentleman who helps her discover it all.

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • Falling Like Snowflakes

    Premiere Date: Saturday, June 29 8/7c

    Cast: Rebecca Dalton, Marcus Rosner

    With the grudging help of her ex, Teagan is determined to capture a photograph of the elusive 12-sided snowflake in the local mountains. They soon find themselves seeking safety in the eye of an oncoming storm and making a new and unexpected connection. 

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • Signed, Sealed, Delivered: A Tale of Three Letters

    Premiere Date: Friday July 12 9/8c

    Cast: Eric Mabius, Kristin Booth, Yan-Kay Crystal Lowe, Geoff Gustafson, Rhiannon Fish.

    After a three-year hiatus, the POstables are back! A Tale of Three Letters follows Oliver (Mabius), Shane (Booth), Rita (Lowe) and Norman (Gustafson) as they work to identify the intended recipients of a trio of dead letters which have a surprising, personal impact on all of them. In addition, Oliver and Shane’s “honeymoon phase” has ended, and their love life may be a part of the story. 

    The movies is the first of two new installments coming to the Signed, Sealed, Deliver franchise. A second film, titled Signed, Sealed, Delivered: To the Moon, will be released in 2025. 

    See how to watch the Signed Sealed Delivered Mysteries in order here. 

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Movies & Mysteries

  • A Very Vermont Christmas

    Premiere Date: Saturday, July 20 8/7c

    Cast:  Katie Leclerc, Ryan McPartlin

    A local champion skier and Vermont brew master teams up with an unlikely match to create a seasonal microbrew in order to save her family’s business by Christmas.

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • His & Hers

    Premiere Date: TBD

    Cast: Lacey Chabert, Brennan Elliott

    Lacey Chabert and Brennan Elliott are teaming up again for their 10th movie together. The longtime co-stars are set to star in His & Hers (working title) as a married couple who work as attorneys at rival New Yokr City firms. When they represent two reality stars heading for divorce, the tensions begin infiltrating their happy home.

    “A chance to reunite with my dear friend, Brennan Elliot and bringing these stories to the audience together is always such a joyful experience. I hope the fans enjoy these new characters!,” Chabert said in a statement. Elliott added: “I have been so blessed and lucky to have been able to star in 10 films with Lacey Chabert! She is a great friend, and collaborating with her on screen has been one of the most treasured and impactful joys of my career.” 

    Chabert and Elliott have starred in four romances together and five films for their Crossword Mysteries franchise. 

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel

  • Santa Tell Me

    Premiere Date: TBD

    Cast: Erin Krakow, Daniel Lissing

    When Calls the Heart‘s Erin Krakow and Daniel Lissing are reuniting on-screen for the first time in six years. The pair will star in a Christmas movie titled Santa Tell Me, which is slated to premiere in late 2024 as part of the Countdown to Christmas lineup. 

    Krakow stars as Elizabeth Thatcher on the hit Western drama, and Lissing played her love interest, Jack Thornton, for the first five seasons. In Season 5, Jack was tragically killed in a landslide just a few days after his and Elizabeth’s wedding. 

    Not many details about the former co-star’s new movie have been revealed, but a first look was released on June 24. In the clip, Krakow and Lissing’s chemistry is as strong as ever.  “May I say, you two make such a lovely couple,” Santa tells their characters.

    Where to Watch: Hallmark Channel
