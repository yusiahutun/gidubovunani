Early life

The actor was born in Philadelphia, USA, on May 17, 1956. He was the son of Benjamin Saget, a supermarket executive, and Rosalyn Saget, a hospital administrator. He grew up in a typical Jewish family, alongside two sisters, Gay and Andrea Saget. He also had two twin brothers, who sadly died at the time of their birth.

As a child, Saget spent a few of his childhood years in Philadelphia before his family moved and settled in Norfolk, Virginia when he was about eight. The comedian received religious education from a young age when he attended the Temple Israel Synagogue in Norfolk.

Saget moved around between various schools. At Norfolk, he attended Larrymore Elementary, Azalea Gardens Middle School, Lake Taylor High, and Rockbridge High School. His family later moved back to Philadelphia, with the comedian completing his high school education at Abington Senior High School.

While in high school, Saget wanted to study medicine and become a doctor. However, his English teacher pushed him to perform arts after seeing how creative he was. So, after graduating from high school, he enrolled at Temple University, pursuing a degree in Film Studies.

Career

Saget’s career in comedy started when he was in college. In 1978, he won a Merit Award at the Students Academy Awards (Student’s Oscars) for a documentary he had created – Through Adam’s Eyes. After graduating from college, he spent several years working as a comedian.

He also landed minor acting roles in films and TV shows. Some of his earlier acting credits include:

  • Devices (short series)
  • Bosom Buddies (TV show)
  • Full Moon High (movie)
  • At Ease (T.V. show)
  • The Greatest American Hero (TV shows)

Saget continued appearing in minor roles in films and TV shows for the better part of the 1980s. However, in 1987, he got his big break when he was featured in the sitcom TV show Full House as Danny Tanner, an acting gig that lasted until 1995. He would later reprise his role as Danny on the reboot TV show Fuller House in 2016.

Wife and children

Bob Saget was a family man in real life, just like his character in Full House. The comedian was married twice, the first time to high school sweetheart Sheri Kramer in 1982. The couple had three children – Lara Melanie Saget, Jennifer Belle Saget, and Aubrey Saget. Unfortunately, the couple divorced in 1997 after 15 years of marriage.

What happened to Bob Saget?

Bob Saget was found dead in his hotel room at the Ritz-Carlton Orlando on January 9, 2022. He was found by hotel staff who checked on him after his family could not contact him. The comedian was on a stand-up comedy tour and had just performed in Ponte Vedra Beach the previous night.
