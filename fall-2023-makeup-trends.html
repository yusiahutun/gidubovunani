The Best Fall 2023 Makeup Trends: Makeup Ideas for Fall

Every product on this page was chosen by a Harper's BAZAAR editor. We may earn commission on some of the items you choose to buy.

Rosdiana Ciaravolo//Getty Images

Latte Makeup

Latte makeup is one of summer's hottest beauty trends that can seamlessly transition into fall (and even winter) months. For those who practically live in exclusively nude tones already, latte makeup should be a no-brainer, since it consists of layering various shades of brown for a monochromatic look.

Latte Makeup

Supermodel Hailey Bieber's sculpted latte take pairs perfectly with her sleek bun and strapless cream dress.

Latte Makeup

Courtesy of Jason Wu

At the Jason Wu Spring/Summer 2024 show, brown-toned eyes took over the runway. Since the theme of the show was shipwreck, makeup artist Diane Kendal elevated the latte-hued eyes with a lip gloss on top to give it a wet, nautical look.
