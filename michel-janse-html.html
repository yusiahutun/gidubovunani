Michel Janse - Age, Family, Bio

YouTuber and actress known for sharing her love of fashion, food, thrifting and interior design. She vlogs about her life including getting divorced at a young age. In 2022, she became well-known when her then-husband, sports journalist Brook Smith, was fired from his job. She has earned more than 220,000 subscribers on her Michel Janse channel.

Before Fame

She earned a Bachelor's Degree in nutrition and dietetics in university. 

Trivia

She spent six years in Los Angeles pursuing acting. She appeared in series like Cupid's Match and the 2015 film My All-American. She struggled with celiac disease and lupus. 

Family Life

In 2019, she married Brook Smith. She moved back home to Texas after they divorced. She began dating musician Jordy Searcy in 2022. They got engaged in July 2023. 

Associated With

She was in My All-American, which starred Aaron Eckhart, Robin Tunney and Finn Wittrock.
