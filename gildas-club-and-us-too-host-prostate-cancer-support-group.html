Gildas Club and Us TOO host prostate cancer support group WHBF OurQuadCities.com

Gilda’s Club Quad Cities and Us TOO Greater Quad Cities have joined forces to support prostate cancer patients here in the QCA. Kelsey Allen, Program Director at Gilda's Club, and Dave and Donna Evens, co-leaders of Us TOO Greater Quad Cities, joined Local 4 to discuss their hybrid prostate cancer support group. For more information, visit our website, OurQuadCities.com.
