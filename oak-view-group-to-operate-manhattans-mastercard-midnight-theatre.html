Oak View Group To Operate Manhattans Mastercard Midnight Theatre

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) — Independent entertainment marketing and production company Dolphin Entertainment announced that Oak View Group has been selected to operate the Manhattan-based concert venue, the Mastercard Midnight Theatre.

The intimate state-of-the-art venue, located between 31st and 33rd Street and 9th and 10th Avenue in Manhattan’s West Plaza can seat 150 fans for a variety of programming, including Broadway, music, improv, comedy, magic, burlesque, spoken word and culinary events.

“We’re excited to bring the talent and expertise of Oak View Group – a company on the very, very short list of leading venue operators worldwide – to the Mastercard Midnight Theatre project. OVG’s involvement validates what we’ve always known – that the Mastercard Midnight Theatre is a truly global destination with unique and captivating fine dining and entertainment. We’re excited for the public to see what they have in store!” stated Bill O’Dowd, CEO of Dolphin Entertainment and Managing Member of the Mastercard Midnight Theatre.

“Mastercard Midnight Theatre is a great opportunity for Oak View Group to bring our expertise in premium hospitality and create exceptional experiences in the heart of one of the culinary capitals of the world. We are excited to be a part of a space focused on what New York City does best-impeccable food, delicious wine, and unparalleled live entertainment,” added Joshua Pell, President Premium Experiences and Global Strategy.
