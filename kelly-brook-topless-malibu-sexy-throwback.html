Kelly Brook strips topless in Malibu for sexy throwback snap The US Sun

KELLY Brook has shared a racy throwback bikini pic which shows the model posing topless while wearing stiletto heels.

The presenter, 40, shared the pic on Instagram with the caption "Malibu 90s".

The throwback pic is a shoutout to her debut calendar shoot 20 years ago on El Matador beach in Malibu, California.
Kelly posed completely starkers for the infamous shoot, which she recreated again a couple of years ago.

Not shy of stripping down to her birthday suit, the model has revealed how her recent two stone weight loss has given her a new found confidence.

Kelly dropped two dress sizes after becoming a SlimFast ambassador in October 2018, but she said it took judgemental strangers pointing out her weight gain to prompt her to make the change.

Kelly told Hello! Magazine: "I feel like my old self again.

"I crept up to a size 16. When my jeans started to feel tight I thought they'd shrunk in the wash.

"I definitely didn't feel my healthiest or most beautiful, and I wanted to feel good again."

Kelly has always embraced her "curves and cellulite" - no matter what the trolls say - but is "feeling fantastic" after embracing a healthier lifestyle.

She started counting calories and hitting the gym to reach her goal weight, as well as working out with her boyfriend Jeremy Parisi. 

But the One Big Happy actress revealed it wasn't just trolls making fun of her weight gain.

Her French model boyfriend Jeremy Parisi nicknamed her a "balloon".

She told The Sun's Fabulous in 2018: "My boyfriend said I looked like a little balloon, it wasn’t just trolls it was him as well.

"I told my mum (Sandra Kelly) she should stop drinking once and she said ‘you should stop eating’.

"My family are quite real with me, I’m not living in some dream world. It was pretty apparent everyone was like ‘what are you going to do about it?'"

She told The Sun Online last month:  "People on the outside noticed my weight gain before I did."

But she also realistic about her new body.

She added: "I'm feeling confident in my body and happy with the weight I am at the minute. I like being curvy, I like having boobs and a bum.

"Like most women, I've got cellulite - I don't look in the mirror and think 'I've done SlimFast so now I'm a Victoria's Secret Model'.

"I just look like a normal 40-year-old woman. I look how I should look, it's not something unattainable.

"I'm never going to have the body I had as a 21-year-old swimwear model and neither would I want to be that girl.
