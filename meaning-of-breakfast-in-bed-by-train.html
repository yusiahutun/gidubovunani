Meaning of Breakfast In Bed by Train

The Train song "Breakfast In Bed" is a whimsical and enchanting track that combines catchy melodies with quirky lyrics. At first listen, one might assume that the song is a light-hearted tribute to the simple pleasure of enjoying a leisurely morning meal in bed. However, upon a closer look at the lyrics, it becomes evident that the song delves into deeper themes of love, desire, and the quest for personal fulfillment.The opening lines of the song set the stage for a journey to the sunny shores of California. While seemingly a reference to the physical beauty of the state, the lyrics also allude to the concept of self-care and the importance of nurturing oneself. The mention of using lotion hints at the need for protection and self-preservation in a world that can be harsh and unforgiving.Moving on to the verse featuring Sidney, the lyrics take on a more intimate tone. The comparison between someone and a flower suggests a delicate and ephemeral beauty. This lyric highlights the longing for connection and exploration of one's desires. It explores the idea that sometimes, one must be vulnerable and open oneself up to experience the richness that life has to offer.As the song continues, we are transported to Norway. This part of the song portrays a sense of adventure and reveals the desire for new and exhilarating experiences. The line "You treat me like a mother loves her baby" adds an unexpected layer to the relationship being described. It speaks to the need for nurturing, care, and emotional support. This theme emphasizes the importance of finding someone who can provide not only excitement but also a sense of comfort and security.The chorus brings the central message of the song to the forefront. The notion that someone can be the best book one has ever read reflects the idea that individuals can provide endless fascination, much like a captivating story. Relationships, just like literature, can offer profound insights and provoke deep emotions. The reference to being "the smartest thing I ever said" speaks to the transformative power of love, suggesting that it can bring out the best in us, allowing us to discover new aspects of our personalities and capabilities.The bridge of the song introduces another surprising metaphor by likening the person in question to breakfast in bed. This imagery conjures feelings of comfort, indulgence, and intimacy. It suggests that this person is not only a source of physical pleasure but also a metaphorical feast, satisfying the hunger for emotional and spiritual nourishment.The final verse reveals the desire to please and take care of the person being sung to. The lyrics suggest a willingness to adapt and be versatile to meet their needs, as seen in the reference to Japanese culture. The mention of loving like a parent loves a child presents a complex mix of emotions: an unconditional love that seeks to protect, nurture, and provide for someone.In conclusion, "Breakfast In Bed" by Train is a charming song that extends beyond its seemingly light-hearted surface. Through whimsical lyrics and unexpected metaphors, the song explores themes of self-care, desire, emotional fulfillment, and personal growth. It reminds us of the transformative power of love and the importance of finding someone who can both thrill and nurture us. So the next time you enjoy a leisurely breakfast in bed, let the song remind you of the boundless possibilities that love and self-discovery can bring.
