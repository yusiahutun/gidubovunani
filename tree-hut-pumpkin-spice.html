Tree Hut's 2023 Pumpkin Spice Scents Are In Stock at Target

Nothing gets me more excited than talking about fall. As a self-proclaimed hot girl (we’re talking temperature, people), I can’t wait until the temps fall below 70 and I can finally wear leggings, socks, sweatshirts, and anything else that screams comfy-cozy. But fall isn’t just about the cute outfits you wear. When I’m in the middle of celebrating this season, I like everything I do to be fall related. That means I’m definitely decorating my apartment, lighting an apple crisp candle, and pre-ordering my Pumpkin Spice Latte on the Starbucks app. It also means every soap and scent I use has to be fall themed as well.

So when I saw that Target was already selling pumpkin spice offerings from Tree Hut, I knew I had to load up my cart immediately. I mean, the annoying thing about fall is how fast fall-related things sell out in stores, amirite? Grab these now to be the early bird who’s stocked up before the world goes pumpkin spice crazy.

  • Tree Hut Pumpkin Spice Latte Body Scrub

    TikTok goes crazy over Tree Hut’s body scrubs. Fans of the product love to show off their bathrooms stocked to the brim with all of their favorite scents. If you pick up just one scent this year, make it this one. The strong scents of pumpkin spice, clove, vanilla, and cinnamon will instantly make you feel like you’re transported to a spa in the coziest fall town. And since it’s just $7.99 you can pick up a few guilt-free.

  • Tree Hut Pumpkin Spice Latte Body Wash

    Pumpkins not only smell delicious, but they’re good for your skin too. This foaming body wash is made with pumpkin enzymes, which is rich in vitamin A and E. They work together to help reveal a smoother looking skin tone and texture.
