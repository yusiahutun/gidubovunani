Everything You Need to Know about UFC Fight Night on ESPN

 Posted on March 15, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  532 words  |  Larita Shotwell

UFC on ESPN 1 is indeed going to be one of the best fight nights ever! While the headline event is certainly Ngannou vs Velasquez, the co-main event is also quite interesting. James Vick vs Paul Felder, where James Vick enters the octagon against Paul Felder is an exciting bout which is bound to thrill the viewers – and might just steal the show!

The battle of the lightweights is going to be quite exciting, noting that both of them faced a loss in their previous matches. There is an extra motivation in James Vick vs Paul Felder because neither of the two wants to damage their reputation by taking two consecutive losses! Let us take a closer look:

James Vick vs Paul Felder: Betting Odds and Predictions

‘The Texecutioner’ James Vick is the leading favourite when it comes to the betting odds by a 53:47 verdict. Data from oddsshark reveals that the odds in the James Vick vs Paul Felder match stand at -110/+102. These are among two of the most equally matched fighters on the card – and are motivated for a win.

Both of these are orthodox MMA fighters. However, the longer the James Vick vs Paul Felder match goes, the more it goes in the favour of Paul Felder. The odds are evenly stacked against both men here. James vs Paul is basically a match between Felder’s kicks vs Vick’s nasty knockout blows.

James Vick vs Paul Felder: Stats

The tale of the tape tells quite an interesting story. James vs Paul is a match between two equals. A closer look a the records of these two fighters reveals that they are truly evenly matched.

James Vick has a win-loss record of 13-2. Compared to him, Paul Felder stands in at 15-4. Interestingly, both these men are squaring off after losing their previous fight. Felder lost to Mike Perry and James Vick lost to Justin Gaethje. However, both these men were on quite a winning streak before that. James Vick had won four successive matches – while Felder had won three.

James Vick, the younger of the two, has an average match time of 8:38 minutes. His lethal knockout blows come as a surprise and can take down any opponent. Moreover, he also has a greater reach when compared to Felder. Paul Felder too is a dangerous and more experienced name. His average match time is over 11 minutes – and the longer it goes, the more it goes in his favour!

James Vick vs Paul Felder: Stream Online, Start Time, Card Info

James Vick vs Paul Felder is the co-main event on the card, where the biggest attraction is the match between Velasquez and Ngannou. Other matches on the main card are Cortney Casey vs. Cynthia Calvillo, Alex Caceres vs. Kron Gracie, Vicente Luque vs. Bryan Barberena and Andre Fili vs. Myles Jury.

Those wanting to watch James Vick vs Paul Felder stream online can easily do so by logging on to the ESPN+ application. Moreover, the viewers based in the US and UK can simply tune in to their ESPN channel at 9PM ET for the main card and at 5:30 PM ET for the preliminary card.
