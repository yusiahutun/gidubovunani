Beyonc strips to dazzling nude illusion bodysuit as she exposes endless legs on stage

Beyoncé looked sensational as she stripped to a bedazzled nude illusion bodysuit covered in fake arms winding their way around her body.

The If I Were A Boy songstress took to the stage in Stockholm, Sweden this week in a clinging gold catsuit covered in sparkles, ending at her upper arms and falling to her ankles.

With Beyoncé’s endless legs on full display, the sequinned ensemble was printed with black-gloved arms with sharp red nails – one curling over Bey’s boobs, another in between her legs and two more trailing up her ankles.
