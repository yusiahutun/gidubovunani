Kate Beckinsale-Pete Davidson PDA Inspires Hilarious Memes

Antoni, sitting beside the love birds at the hockey game, soon became a symbol of what it's like to be a third wheel.

Kate Beckinsale and "SNL" star Pete Davidson's public make out session at a New York Rangers hockey game on Sunday night has gotten the meme treatment -- and the results are hilarious.

The meme comedians focused on "Queer Eye" star Antoni Porowski, who was sitting next to the love birds, as he desperately attempted to ignore the outrageous PDA happening right beside him.
