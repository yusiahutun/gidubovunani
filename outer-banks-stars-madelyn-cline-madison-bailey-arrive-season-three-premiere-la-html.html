Outer Banks stars Madelyn Cline and Madison Bailey arrive at season three premiere in LA

Outer Banks stars Madelyn Cline and Madison Bailey dazzled as they arrived at the premiere for season three of the hit Netflix series in Los Angeles on Thursday night.

Cline, 25 - who plays the show's female lead, Sarah Cameron - wowed shutterbugs in a baby blue dress covered in sparkly blue crystals.

Meanwhile, Bailey - who plays Kiara - stunned onlookers in a green metallic dress with sheer black mesh that teased her toned figure. 

Cline reunited on the red carpet with her co-star and ex-boyfriend Chase Stokes, who she split from in late 2021 after more than a year together.

Outer Banks follows adventurous teen John B (Stokes) who rallies his three best friends to help him hunt for a 'legendary treasure linked to his father's disappearance,' as per Netflix.

Dazzling divas: Outer Banks stars Madelyn Cline and Madison Bailey dazzled as they made their grand arrival at the premiere for season three of the hit Netflix series in Los Angeles on Thursday night

All 10 episodes of season three will be available to stream starting Thursday, February 23.

Cline's slinky number highlighted her hourglass frame and featured soft ruffling towards the bottom. 

She let her dress steal the show by going without accessories and opting for a soft glam makeup look.

Her golden hair flowed down onto her shoulders in straightened strands.

She stepped along the red carpet, which was covered in sand as an homage to the show's island setting, in transparent sandals.

Cline is now romantically linked to singer Jackson Guthy, 26, though she's yet to go public with the relationship.

She recently told TODAY that she is constantly considering 'what not to share with the world, and what to keep personal' following her public breakup with Stokes.

Stokes reaffirmed his heartthrob status by showing off his chiseled chest in an unbuttoned black dress shirt.

Cute smile: The Netflix star flashed her cute smile upon arrival at the premiere in the Westwood area

The 30-year-old actor let a gold chain hang down onto his pecs and layered up with a stylish black blazer.

He slipped on tight black pants and slicked his dark brown hair back to complete his red carpet look.

Stokes recently praised his former girlfriend Cline, calling her a 'great actress' and revealing they both agreed to 'always put the work first.' 

'Truth be told, she's an incredible actress. She's a lovely human being,' he told PEOPLE.

'And I'm over the moon and just super proud of the work that we've done over the past three seasons. I'm always going to root for her.'

Stokes is currently 'having a good time' with country songstress Kelsea Ballerini, 29, who he gushed to the outlet is a 'lovely lady.' 

Their buddy romance comes nearly six months after Ballerini split from husband Morgan Evans, 37.
