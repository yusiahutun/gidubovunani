What is Victoria Ruffo Net Worth 2023? Bio | Age 2023 and Family

Mexican actress, Victoria Ruffo came to the spotlight for her roles as Macarena Espinoza in In the Name of Love (2008), María Fernández Acuña in Stepmother (2005), and Victoria Sandoval in Triunfo del Amor (2010). She is also famous as the wife of a Mexican politician and Lawyer, Omar Fayad Meneses.

Early Life of Victoria Ruffo

Victoria Ruffo opened her eyes to this beautiful world on May 31, 1961, in Mexico City, Mexico. As of 2023, her age is 59 years old and her zodiac sign is Taurus. Every year, she celebrates her birthday on the 31st of May. Her full name is María Victoria Eugenia Guadalupe Martínez del Río Moreno-Ruffo.

Victoria’s father’s name is Ramon Martinez del Río and her mother’s name is Guadalupe Moreno Herrera. She grew up with her other two sisters, Gabriel Ruffo, and Marcela Ruffo. By nationality, she holds Mexican citizenship and belongs to a white ethnic background. Moreover, she follows Christianity as a religion.

Victoria Ruffo’s Married Life

Victoria is married to a Mexican politician and Lawyer, Omar Fayad Meneses. The couple tied their wedding knot on March 9, 2001. Victoria and Omar are the parents of twins one daughter and one son. Their children’s names are Victoria and Anuar. The twins were born on 11th August 2004.

Before marrying Omar, Victoria was married to a comedian, Eugenio Derbez. Victoria and Eugenio exchanged their wedding vows in 1992 but their relationship didn’t last long and divorced in 1997. The couple had one son named Jose Eduardo Derbez born in 1992.
