Who is Sarai Burgos?

Sarai Burgos is a popular celebrity kid, renowned for being the child of the illustrious American model and businessperson, Bernice Burgos. Her mother, Bernice is a popular social media influencer with 7 million followers on Instagram.

Sarai Burgos, who was born in the Bronx, New York in the year 2006, is reportedly 10 years younger than her elder sister, Ashley Burgos. When Ashley was born, their mother Bernice Burgos, was still a teen. Evidently, Bernice Burgos faced some struggles in her task of nurturing both Sarai and Ashley.

During an interview, Bernice Burgos discussed how at 15 years old, she was kicked out from her grandmother’s house and had to live with her then-boyfriend. As a result, she gave birth to her daughter Sarai Burgos, the older sister of Ashley Burgos.
