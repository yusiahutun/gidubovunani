Adorable moment three-year-old girl plays on a laptop in Canada [video]

This is the sweet moment a three-year-old girl played on a laptop and told her family she needed space because she was 'on a meeting.'

Footage filmed by Karalea Pior in Toronto, Canada, shows her daughter Franki in her 'Monday meetings' yesterday.

Franki looks at the camera and tells her mother that she has a meeting as she taps away at the laptop.

Footage filmed by Karalea Pior in Toronto, Canada, shows her daughter Franki in her 'Monday meetings' yesterday

Her commentary continues as she says  'K, we gotta go though' and points at her sister Stevie, saying she needs space because of her meeting.

Looking at the screen, she greets 'Sarah' and tells her it is a beautiful day.

The tone gets a little bit ominous after a few seconds as she says 'Sarah, blink twice if you need help!'

But she starts laughing with her mother and then says: 'NO! I still have a meeting.'

The video has gone viral, racking up more than 350,000 views since it was posted and 66,000 likes.  

After the slightly ominous commentary she then laughs with her mother and says 'What???'

Many viewers picked up on the unusual 'blink twice' line, which her mother explained in the comments.

Karalea said had told her other daughter: 'Stevie blink twice if you need help' a month ago and Franki had been randomly saying the phrase ever since.  

The sweet clip is one of many that Karalea and her husband Swav post of their family on TikTok. 

Mrs Pior, 34, also has an Instagram account where she has racked up thousands of followers by sharing clips and snippets of the family-of-four's life together.
