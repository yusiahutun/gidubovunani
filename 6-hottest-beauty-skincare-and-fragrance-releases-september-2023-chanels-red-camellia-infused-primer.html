6 hottest beauty, skincare and fragrance releases in September 2023: from Chanels red camellia-in

1. Byredo

Byredo and Susanne Kaufmann have come together to unveil a limited-edition collection that embodies their shared passion for natural beauty, aiming to captivate the senses while nurturing the skin. The result? A luxurious scented body oil and a roll-on fragrance oil, both enriched with skincare benefits and a deeply moisturising formula.

How to protect your skin from UV rays and sun damage on flights

2. Chanel Beauty

Elevate your beauty routine with the N°1 De Chanel Red Camellia Skin Enhancer, which is enriched with the potent extract and oil of the flower. Whether used alone for a fresh and natural look or as a skin-perfecting primer under your foundation, it will boost radiance as the formula is infused with nourishing plant waxes and hyaluronic acid for that smooth complexion.

3. Gucci Beauty

Brighten up your skin with one of Gucci Beauty’s newest creations, the Illuminateur de Beauté. This liquid highlighter promises an instant boost of hydration, giving your face a fresh, radiant glow. It can be applied on bare skin or mixed into foundation or a primer.

4. Oribe

Founded in 2008 by industry veteran Daniel Kaner and two business partners including renowned hairstylist Oribe Canales, the brand set out on a mission to create a collection of luxurious hair care products unlike anything else on the market.

To commemorate its 15th anniversary, Oribe introduces a limited-edition anniversary set featuring 15 travel-sized items as a testament to the brand’s dedication when it comes to hair care experiences.

Sunscreen isn’t enough, experts say – you need a sun-protective wardrobe too

5. Laura Mercier

Known for its exceptional setting powders, Laura Mercier welcomes its first talc-free setting powder to the brand’s collection. Infused with ingredients like amino acid and silica powders, the new formula delivers total shine control and zero flashback while effortlessly blurring the appearance of pores.

6. Tom Ford

Go on an enchanting olfactory adventure with Tom Ford’s latest creation, the alluring Myrrhe Mystère fragrance from its renowned Private Blend collection. The perfume combines the richness of myrrh elements with an indulgent ultra-vanilla accord that is encased in the iconic Private Blend bottle with a brown plaque.
