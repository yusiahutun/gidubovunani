Ant McPartlin's luxury 100k wedding reception venue revealed as glamorous hotel

Ant McPartlin has preparations completely underway in the lead-up to his wedding to Anne-Marie Corbett tomorrow in Hampshire.

The pair are set to marry tomorrow in a glamorous reception with their closest friends and family, with 100 people set to be in attendance.

The TV presenter is reported to have the wedding reception at Heckfield Place in Hampshire with 100 of Ant and Anne-Marie's closest friends.

Heckfield Place is a glamorous location with gardens, meadows and woodland surrounding the area - but it comes with a hefty price tag with Ant reportedly paying a whopping £100,000 for his friends and family to attend.

The Mirror has reported that guests will start to arrive this evening and stay in the luxury location for two nights, making the most of their weekend.

With a luxury feel all around the grounds and glamorous rooms with fire places indoors, it looks like the couple have picked the perfect stay for their dream wedding.

Although they're not planning on taking the guest list to the extreme, it's looking like there will be a handful of famous faces in attendance including the likes of Holly Willoughby, Philip Schofield and, of course, Declan Donnelly.
