What is Jeff Goldblum’s net worth? Who is Jeff Goldblum’s wife?

Introduction

Jeff Goldblum is an American actor and musician best known for having starred in such films as Independence Day and Jurassic Park.

Biography

He was born on October 22, 1952, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, USA, as Jeffrey Lynn Goldblum.

His father, Harold, was a doctor, while his mother, Shirley Jane, was a radio broadcaster.

Jeff has a sister, Pamela, and two older brothers, Lee and Rick.

His brother, Rick, passed away in 1971 due to kidney failure.

”He was a great guy, and I was very close to him,” Jeff told Standard in April 2012. ”But everybody has loss in their life. We all lose everything eventually – family, looks, youth, career and relationships, even the best ones.”

In the late 60s, Jeff attended summer sessions at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh.

© Getty Images

Goldblum made his Broadway debut in the musical ”Two Gentlemen of Verona.”

”My acting teacher Sandy Meisner said it takes 20 years to call yourself an actor and a lifetime of being a student,” Goldblum said in November 2019.

He made his film debut in the 1974 Charles Bronson film ”Death Wish.”

In 1986, Goldblum starred in The Fly as Seth Brundle, a scientist who slowly turns into a fly-hybrid creature. The film was directed by David Cronenberg.

In 1993, he performed as Dr. Ian Malcolm in Jurassic Park, the first installment in the Jurassic Park franchise.

© Getty Images

It was followed by the sequels:

  • The Lost World: Jurassic Park (1997);
  • Jurassic Park III (2001);
  • Jurassic World (2015);
  • Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom (2018).

In 1996, Jeff portrayed a New York satellite technician David Levinson, who discovers an alien plan to attack Earth in the sci-fi/action film Independence Day, co-starring Will Smith. The film was directed by Roland Emmerich. It grossed over $800 million worldwide.

In 2017, he was cast in the Marvel Comics Cinematic Universe, appearing in Thor: Ragnorak as The Grandmaster — the eccentric ruler of Sakaar who places prisoners in deadly battles against his champion gladiator, Hulk.

In 2018, Jeff and the Mildred Snitzer Orchestra released their debut studio album, ”The Capitol Studio Sessions.” Guest performers include Sarah Silverman, Haley Reinhart, Imelda May, and Till Brönner. Note – he has been playing the piano since he was a child.

In August 2018, Jeff appeared in the season finale of the First We Feast series with Sean Evans, where he has eaten vegan hot wings.

In 2019, Goldblum hosted The World According to Jeff Goldblum, a documentary streamed on Disney+. Jeff said that the series reflects ”my appetite for spontaneity and just mixing it up with real people.”

In 2020, Jeff was the voice of Dr. Ian Malcolm in the video game Jurassic World Aftermath.

Wife

On July 4, 1980, Jeff married Patricia Gaul, an actress known for Some Kind of Wonderful and Road Trip.

© Getty Images

They appeared together in Silverado.

The couple divorced in 1985.

On November 1, 1987, Goldblum married Geena Davis, an actress who is the recipient of a Golden Globe Award and an Academy Award.

© Getty Images

The two first met while filming Transylvania 6-5000.

Jeff and Geena divorced in 1991.

From 1995 to 1997, Goldblum was in a relationship with Laura Dern.

© Getty Images

They first met during the filming of Spielberg’s ”Jurassic Park” (1993), a role that Dern reprised in the sequel, ”Jurassic Park III.”

”I was struck from the beginning,” Goldblum said in an interview about Laura. ”But after the movie, we realized we liked each other.”

On November 8, 2014, Jeff Goldblum married Emilie Livingston at the Chateau Marmont in Los Angeles.

© Getty Images

Emilie is a retired Olympic rhythmic gymnast who represented Canada at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney, Australia.

The couple began dating in 2011.

”Jeff always makes me feel loved from the first day we met,” Livingston said in June 2018. ”At first, I wondered if this feeling could be true because I hadn’t experienced such innocent, passionate, tender, honest love before so quickly.”

So, how much is Jeff Goldblum worth?

Goldblum also made money from appearing in ads for Toyota, Procter & Gamble, Apple, the National Lottery, and apartments.com.
