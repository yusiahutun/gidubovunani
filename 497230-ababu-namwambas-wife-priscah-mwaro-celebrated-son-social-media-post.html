Ababu Namwamba's Ex-Wife Priscah Mwaro Celebrated Son on Her Last Social Media Post

  • Priscah and Ababu Namwamba were blessed with three children, and they always posted them online during celebrations such as birthdays
  • The mother of three shared a post eight weeks ago, celebrating her son before she was found dead at her Nairobi home
  • The Sports Cabinet Secretary and his wife are said to have parted ways a few years ago and were co-parenting, though he lived with the kids

Priscah Mwaro, the ex-wife of Cabinet Secretary for Youth Affairs, Sports and the Arts Ababu Namwamba, passed away a day after celebrating her birthday.

Priscah Mwaro, the loving mum

Priscah and Ababu were blessed with three children, a daughter and two sons.

The couple's kids live with Ababu, who is always sharing photos and videos of them on social media.

On January 10, Priscah shared a video of her lastborn son playing play station on Instagram, which was her last post.

"Lovely," she captioned the video.

In 2021, the mother of three shared photos of her children on different dates, pampering them with love, and in love, she congratulated her firstborn daughter after she was voted a leader of her class.

"Congratulations baby girl,#MemberOfParliamentBlessings Galore," she wrote.

On April 16, 2020, the loving mother celebrated her lastborn on his birthday with a heartwarming message which read:

"Happy birthday, my lastborn... You are a gift from our Heavenly Father, and I believe that after God made you my son, he must have stood back and said, 'Wow, I rock!' Happy birthday to an awesome creation."

Priscah lived life to the fullest, and in one of her posts dated March 17, 2021, she posted a photo posing alongside two other people and captioned it:

"Life is short...enjoy while it lasts."

Before her death, Priscah was not active on social media as before and rarely shared much.

Ababu Namwamba and Priscah Mwaro's happy times

Earlier TUKO.co.ke featured photos of Ababu and Priscah during their happy times.

The duo had a picturesque relationship before they started having issues in 2018.

The two former lovebirds started dating when they were still pretty young.

A throwback photo shared by Prisca in 2015 showed them smiling at the camera as Namwamba held her from the back. From their dressing, the image was from the early 2000s.

The CS and his ex-wife were once considered one of Kenya’s most lovely couples.

In 2016, they were featured on the cover of Drum Magazine. In the cover image, the two looked happier than ever before.
