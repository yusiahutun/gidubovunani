Nicole Sullivan Bio, Career, Boyfriend, Affair, Married, Fact, Height, Weight &amp; Net worth

Short Description of Nicole Sullivan:

Nicole Sullivan is an American actress, comedian and voice artist who is famous for her six seasons on the comedy series named MADtv. She is also known for playing the role of Holly Shumpert for the five seasons on the CBS sitcom The King of Queens.

Furthermore, she is also known for her role as Jill Tracy on Scrubs. She has also voiced the Shego in Disney’s Kim Possible.

Nicole Sullivan’s Early Life and Education

Nicole Julianne Sullivan aka Nicole Sullivan was born on April 21, 1970, in New York, U.S. She holds the American nationality and belongs to White American ethnicity. When she was just seven years old she took dance classes.

Her mother is a famous businesswoman and her father represented Manhattan ‘s 69th Assembly District in the New York State Assembly. She was a class treasurer throughout her junior year in her high school.

After the completion of her high school education, she attended Northwestern University as a theatre major. She spent her junior year in London where she studied at the British American Drama Academy.

Nicole Sullivan’s Career

The proficient actress was cast as Turanga Leela one of the main roles in the animated series named Futurama. Later the role was recast with Katey Sagal.

She has also voiced Marlene the Otter on The Penguins of Madagascar from the famous movies Madagascar, Madagascar: Escape 2 Africa and Madagascar 3: Europe’s Most Wanted.

Additionally, she appeared in Meet the Robinsons with a role of Franny Robinson. She was also a voice actor in the Monsters, Inc. video game. She featured the role of Luna in the 2007 video game Ratchet & Clank: Size Matters.

Nicole Sullivan’s Personal Life
