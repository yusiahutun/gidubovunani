Mother of four killed in Ellisville DUI wreck | Local News

A drunk man who registered nearly twice the legal limit is charged with killing a mother of four in a horrific accident Thursday evening on Highway 11 near the Jones County Adult Detention Center.

Jose Israel Gabriel, 36, kept his head down throughout the short court proceeding in front of Judge Howell Beech and on occasion wiped away tears. Through an interpreter, Beech set bond at $500,000 — cash only. Gabriel is charged with DUI causing the death of another, having no driver’s license and no insurance.
