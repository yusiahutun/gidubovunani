Zazie Beetz’s Bio (Age)

Born on June 1, 1991 in Charite, Berlin-Mitte, Germany, Zazie was christened Zazie Olivia Beetz by her German father and African American mother. Her father Thomas was born in East Germany, but he emigrated to the United States after the fall of the Berlin Wall and met his wife Michelle, a social worker in the United States.

Zazie’s elementary school education was in Berlin, although she grew up in Uptown Manhattan, New York and earned a French degree from Skidmore College. The actress’ flair for acting was born when she was in elementary school, where she participated in community theater and local stages. She later trained at a number of theater institutes, including the Harlem School of Arts and LaGuardia Arts High School.

Before her acting debut, she was an employee at the Alexa Center, works at its H & M store. Once she retired from the business to focus on her acting career, she landed a role in the short film. Crocotta (2013). The following year she starred in the short film Beasts (2014), which later paved the way for bigger roles like her roles in films like Apple Sauce (2015), Wolves (2016), Geostorm (2017), Dead Pigs (2018), Dead Pool (2018) and Joker (2019).

Besides acting in films, Zazie has also taken part in quite a few television series and shows. She starred in the series Atlanta, Easy and more. She has won multiple awards for her collection for her amazing performances on set. She was nominated for a Choice Award for Choice Summer Movie Actress in 2018 for her performance in Deadpool 2 and Prime Time Emmy Awards nominated her for her performance in the comedy series Atlanta.
