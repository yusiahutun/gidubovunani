Who Is Rande Gerber Married To?

Rande Gerber is married to Cindy Crawford, a supermodel since 1998.

The couple first met in 1991. They met each other at the wedding of Michael Gruber. Gruber was Gerber’s childhood friend as well as Crawford’s agent. Crawford was married to Richard Gere at that time.

After their first encounter, Gerber and Cindy remained in touch over the phone. Crawford already had divorced Gere before she got reconnected with Gerber in 1995 in Los Angeles.

Crawford and Gerber started dating officially in 1997. The couple tied a knot in May 1998 in an intimate wedding ceremony.

Crawford was seen wearing a John Galliano mini lace shift dress, carrying a casual bouquet of white flowers, and rocked effortless beach waves in their relaxed beach wedding. After the wedding, the newlyweds Garber and Cindy swapped their casual beach attire for bathing suits and sealed the marriage with a jump in the pool.

In the course of their married life, the duo became the parents of two kids. They welcomed a son, Presley walker (2 July 1998), and daughter Kaia Jordan (3 September 2001).

Who Is Rande Gerber? Parents Details

American entrepreneur Rande Gerber was born on 27 April 1962 in New York City, New York. He has successfully celebrated 58 birthdays of his life.

He was born to parents Jordan Gerber and Ellen Peckman. He was raised and grown up with his three siblings. Kenny Gerber and Scott Gerber are two brothers of Rande whereas, Sophie is the only sister. Both of his brothers are the co-founder of the Gerber group.

Rande Gerber completed his graduation level from George W. Hewlett High School, Long Island, in 1980. Later he joined the University of Arizona and graduated with a BA in Marketing.

Concerning his physical appearance, Rande weighs around 80 kg (176 Ibs) and stands at the height of 6 feet and 1 inch (1.85 m).
