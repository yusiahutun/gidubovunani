Molly Sims, 45, Looks Incredible In Red Bathing Suit While On Holiday Vacation With Family See T

Molly Sims is a model and an actress, probably best known for starring in the television series Las Vegas from 2003-2008. Aside from that, the 45-year-old is also known for keeping herself in great shape even as the years go by. Most recently, the mother-of-three decided to take a holiday break with her family to St. Barts where she showed off her bikini body.

Molly, her husband, 50-year-old Scott Stuber, and their three kids: 6-year-old Brooks, 3-year-old Scarlett, and 1-year-old Grey, hit the Caribbean beach to take in the hot weather and fantastic sights during their Christmas break. But one sight that was turning heads was Molly in her red bathing suit. The Kentucky native also sported some sunglasses to add to the whole look.

In fact, a little while ago Molly told Closer how it is that she stays so fit. “Food is protein and vegetables. I do drink, I drink wine,” Molly explained, adding that when it comes to alcohol and sugar, “You have to cut it out at some point. You can’t just — I mean, I don’t cut it out, but I definitely limit it.” Molly added, “I am a big believer in wellness and taking care of yourself, and I do believe that you’re your own best advocate. I research, I find things, I try things, I get talked into things — but it’s good, and I like different things!”
