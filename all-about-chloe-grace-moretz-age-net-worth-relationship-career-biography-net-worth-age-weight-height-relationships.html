All About Chloe Grace Moretz

 Posted on July 24, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  560 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Actress Chloe Grace Moretz is originally from the United States. Her best-known roles are in the films “The Poker House,” “Kick-Ass,” and “If I Say.”

Her role in the film “The Amityville Horror” earned her a major award nomination. She made her acting debut in Hollywood as a child.

Where was Chloe Grace Moretz born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Chloe was born in Atlanta, Georgia, USA on February 10, 1997. She is currently 23 years old. Her parents are Teri Duke and McCoy Moretz. McCoy is a plastic surgeon while Teri is a nurse practitioner. Cartersville, Georgia served as Chloe’s foster parent. Her four older brothers are Ethan, Colin, Brandon, and Trevor.

Is Chloe Grace Moretz Single? Relationship

Actor Cameron Fuller and Chloe were in a relationship from 2012 to 2014. She then dated Brooklyn Beckham from 2014 to 2018, however, their relationship ended halfway through.

Chloe Grace Moretz was rumored to have dated Alex Roe in 2017 and Neymar Jr. in 2016. She was spotted with Kate Harrison in California in 2018, sparking rumors. Chloe considers herself a feminist and has actively supported LGBT equality.

How tall is Chloe Grace Moretz? Weight, Hair Color

Chloe Grace Moretz is a Caucasian woman with American citizenship. She has Swiss-German, Scottish, and Welsh ancestry in addition to her German and English heritage.

How did Chloe Grace Moretz begin her professional career?

Moretz appeared in two episodes of the television series “The Guardian” in 2004 before making her film debut in 2005 with the film “Heart of the Beholder.”

She received another role the following year in “The Amityville Horror.” In 2010, she played Hit-Girl in the action film “Kick-Ass” after making brief appearances in several other films. Finally, in 2011, she was cast in the murder drama “Texas Killing Fields” as Little Anne Sliger.

Chloe began her career on Broadway with her film career in the 2014 off-Broadway show “The Library.” She eventually made an appearance as Shelby in the 2016 comedy “Neighbors 2: Sorority Rising.”

The following year, she made appearances in both the comedy-drama “I Love You, Daddy” and the crime drama “November Criminals.” Later, she appeared as the lead in the drama film “The Miseducation of Cameron Post.” 2018 saw the release of her horror film “Suspiria.”

She voiced The Addams Family in the CGI animated film and starred in the thriller Greta the following year. The next film she will appear in is Tom and Jerry, which is set to be released in March 2021, as well as Shadow in the Cloud.
