Stephanie Thirkill and her baby daddy Richie Myler share a lot in common. For instance, they both come from broken marriages. Yes! Stephanie’s married before too.

So, who’s her husband (now ex)? What’s her net worth and age? Find answers and more as this article proceeds.

Stephanie Thirkill Husband

For those of you confused, Richie Myler isn’t Stephanie Thirkill’s husband.

The two first met at a rugby dinner in late 2021. Since then, they dated undercover until Richie’s then-wife Helen Skelton suspected infidelity and got in touch with a detective to help uncover the truth.

However, Richie insisted that was no crossover and that he began the romance with Stephanie after their marriage collapsed.

Eventually, Richie and Helen announced their split in April 2022 — after 9 years of marriage. Writing with a heavy heart, Helen took it to her IG to share, “Very sad to say Richie and I are no longer a couple. He has left the family home. We will be doing our best to co-parent our small children.”

Now, coming back to Stephanie and Richie, after dating for a while, the two were first spotted together enjoying a romantic stroll in May 2022. For the walk, Stephanie donned an oversized red jumper and patterned leggings while Richie sported an all-black ensemble with a pair of shorts.

Later, in July, the Rugby player finally made their relationship IG official when they attended the Wimbledon tennis championships together.

Fast-forward to October of that year, Stephanie and Richie also made another headline sharing that they were expecting their first child together. A source mentioned that the pair was “thrilled” about their pregnancy and “extremely happy together”.

Also, it was revealed that Richie broke the news to Helen, the mother of his three kids, before the social media post.

“Stephanie and Richie are obviously thrilled. They have moved very quickly but are extremely happy together. The timing of the pregnancy may raise a few eyebrows though,” a friend of Richie shared.

Getting your girlfriend pregnant just after 6 months of a split from your wife would certainly “raise a few eyebrows.”

Who Is Stephanie Thirkill Ex Husband?

Stephanie Thirkill married her ex-husband whose name is also Richie back on November 9, 2018. Their lavish wedding was held at Bolton Abbey, the Yorkshire estate of the Duke of Devonshire.

Sadly, the pair broke up just a few years later.

On the other hand, Stephanie’s now boyfriend, Richie Myler was married to Helen Skelton for 8 years. The former husband/wife tied the knot in December 2013 after meeting just two years earlier. They then welcomed three children — Ernie (6), Louis (5), and four-month-old Elsie Kate (ages as of 2022) and shared a home in France for two years before relocating to Leeds.

Unfortunately, they separated in October 2022.

The news about their split also came just hours after Helen shared a post about the stresses of motherhood. It read, “A mama is always full of emotions, full of love, full of anxiety and worries, full of exhaustion and lack of energy, full of mum guilt and doubt, full of pride in her kids, full of pure joy.”

Back in 2018, when Helen was asked about the secret to a happy marriage. She replied, “’We only married in 2013 – so ask me the secret in 10 years’ time.” However, she did mention that marriages and relationships are not all “plain sailing, but we approach it like teamwork.”

“Rich is the best dad. He’s the ‘sugar police’ when it comes to their diet and insists on routine, whereas sometimes I’m anything for a quiet life, so we’re a good balance,” she explained.

Helen Skelton is a TV presenter, motivational speaker, athlete, and actor. You might have seen her on TV shows such as Blue Peter and Countryfile. Raised on a dairy farm outside Carlisle, Cumbria, England, she went on to study journalism at the Cumbria Institute of the Arts.
