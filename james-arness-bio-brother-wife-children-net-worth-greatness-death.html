James Arness’ Bio

Born James King Aurness on May 26,1923 in Minneapolis, USA, the late actor dropped the later “u” in his last name after becoming an actor. His father, Rolf Cirkler Aurness, worked as a businessman, while his mother, Ruth Duesler, made ends meet as a journalist. He is the first of his parents’ two children and has a younger brother named Peter Graves. What looks like a united family all fell apart after his parents divorced in the 1940s, leaving James in a broken state.

Regarding his education, James Arness attended John Burroughs Elementary School before going to Washburn High School. He later transferred to West High, where he graduated in 1942. After graduation, the actor took a side job. He worked as a loader and courier in a jewelry store.

Before becoming an actor, James Arness had always dreamed of being a naval fighter pilot, however he built limitations in his heart to prevent his dreams from being fulfilled. Despite this, he later joined the US Army and served from 1943 to 1945 before retiring after being seriously injured at the Battle of Anzio.

After leaving the army, James Arness took a job as a radio announcer; While there, he decided to join the acting world and also changed his last name. He made his film debut in the film The Farmer’s Daughter in 1947 and also landed another role in the crime thriller; Roses are red . His remarkable on-set performances saw his roles steadily increase, and by the time he was forty-six on the screen he had appeared in over 38 films.

In television series, James Arness had a long-running role on the series Gunsmoke , wowing audiences in each of the episodes before it closed in 1975. Before that he worked between schedules and acted in a few other television series including The Lone Ranger (1950), Lux Video Theater (1954), The Red Skelton Chevy Special (1959) and the rest of them.

As an author, James Arness published his autobiography entitled James Arness: An Autobiography Published in September 2001 with a foreword by Burt Reynolds. When it comes to accolades James has not been left out, he has received quite a few accolades and has also been acclaimed for his role in the drama series Gunsmoke.

Family – brother, wife and children
