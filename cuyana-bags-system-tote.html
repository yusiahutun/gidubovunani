Cuyana Just Debuted a New Work Bag, the System Tote

In our new world of hybrid work, a chic carry-all bag is still a must—even for toting a laptop to the office once per week. Cuyana, the maker of best-selling totes worn by Meghan Markle and Jessica Alba, just released a work bag for those intermittent returns to HQ and more: the System Tote.

From the outside, the System Tote has all the same qualities that distinguish Cuyana as one of BAZAAR.com’s favorite direct-to-consumer brands. This tote features a streamlined top handle silhouette and supple, lightweight leather in six neutral tones—a minimalist look meant for everyday use. Cuyana is also sustainability-minded, so the System Tote is made from responsibly-sourced leather.

Cuyana System Tote

Cuyana System Tote

Credit: Cuyana

But the key word here is system. The tote is actually a 3-in-1 bag, able to carry a detachable laptop case and smaller flap pouch bag. Each of these pieces comes in coordinating shades with the tote, and each can snap into discrete closures within the bag or stand on their own. (With an adjustable strap, the flap bag looks just like the ‘90s mini bags appearing all over street style.) There are 13” and 16” versions of both the tote and the laptop case—so Mac and PC women can partake.

All of the System Tote’s interchangeable pieces arrive as we demand versatility from our work lives (and our accessories). “Throughout the pandemic, the needs of our customers have drastically evolved,” Cuyana co-founder and CEO Karla Gallardo tells BAZAAR.com. “Our System Tote is guaranteed to take you wherever you need to be—whether it is running a quick errand, grabbing drinks, or heading back to the office.”

So yes, the System Tote is destined to become a go-to work bag—but it’s also an anywhere bag. Wherever you’re toting it, you can select a 3-in-1 system exactly catered to your schedule below or at Cuyana.
