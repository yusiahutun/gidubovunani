Taylor Swift Throws a 4th of July Party

Ain't no party like a Taylor Swift party. The singer is known for her just-because get-togethers, but give the girl an occasion and she'll show you a soirée like you've never seen.

To start off the long holiday weekend, Taylor Swift threw a Fourth of July party with her whole crew: Calvin Harris, Ed Sheeran, Martha Hunt, Serayah, Brit Maack, Cody Lewis, the Haim sisters, Gigi Hadid and Joe Jonas were all in attendance. It looks pretty major to say the least. Swift checked off all the boxes for an epic summertime bash: best friends (and boyfriends), BBQ, bikinis, beach floaties, and plenty of patriotic red, white and blue.
