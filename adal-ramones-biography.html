BIOGRAPHY
Adalberto Javier Ramones Martínez, known simply as Adal Ramones , is a businessman, host and television producer, born on December 3, 1961, in Monterrey, Nuevo León, Mexico. Graduated from the Regiomontana University, with a degree in communication sciences, Adal began his career in entertainment as a producer of theatrical activities for the musical magazine La apple de la discordia . He moved to Mexico City, where he began working as a producer and director of commercials and series such as Qué clowns and El Espacio de Cositas . By the mid-90s, Ramones would become a celebrity when he premiered his show Otra Roll , which would become the highest-rated show of the time thanks to the renowned guests on the show, including Bon Jovi. , Britney Spears, Sylvester Stallone, N’Sync, Elton John, Will Smith, Maradona, Arnold Schwarzenegger, among several others. The program came to an end in 2007. Ramones has been the host of programs such as Bailando por un Sueño , Cantando por un Sueño , Los reyes de la pista , among others. As a fictional actor, he has participated in series such as Sr. Ávila (2013) and Por amar sin ley (2018), as well as films such as No manches Frida (2016), Hasta que la wedding nos separe (2018) and A pirate dad ( 2019).
